## Paper Outline

### Structure Pattern: Theoretical Analysis (hybrid with empirical testing)

The paper makes a theoretical argument (unified three-layer ontological framework) and tests it against two empirical domains (international law and COVID-19 biopolitics). This is a **Theoretical + Case Study hybrid** — the theoretical core (§1–4) drives the argument, with two empirical anchoring sections (§5) demonstrating analytical purchase, followed by critical self-reflection (§6).

### Overview

The paper argues that Zevnik's "void," Bonta/Protevi's "plane of immanence," and Žižek's "the Real" are not competing concepts occupying the same theoretical space, but three distinct layers of a unified ontological project: void as the pre-ontological affirmative ground, plane of immanence as the structured topological space of virtual attractors, and the Real as the anti-ontological surplus-effect produced by every act of symbolization. The argument proceeds from the most abstract (void) to the most concrete (empirical cases), then loops back to self-critique (§6) and an application prospectus (conclusion).

### Detailed Outline

#### 引言 Introduction (~1,500 words)

**Purpose**: Frame the core puzzle — three independent philosophical traditions (Lacanian, Deleuzian, post-Deleuzian complexity theory) converge on the same question of "ultimate ontological support" but give three different answers. State the thesis (they form a three-layer structured answer, not competitors). Preview the argument. Situate the paper within the broader Lacan-Deleuze debate (Nedoh & Zevnik 2016; Nedoh 2019).

**Content**:
- Research puzzle and stakes: why ontology matters for political analysis
- Three traditions, three concepts, one wrong way to read them
- Thesis: a three-layer unified model
- Article roadmap
- *Serves sub-question*: framing (no sub-question binding)

**Sources**: Zevnik 2016 [E10], Nedoh & Zevnik 2016 [N4], Nedoh 2019 [N12], Molloy 2017 [N3], Heron 2019 [N13]

**Transition to §1**: Why start with the void — the most foundational layer.

---

#### 1. 虚空：前本体论的肯定性"无" (~1,800 words)

**Purpose**: Establish the void as the affirmative nothingness that grounds all possibility of order, drawing primarily on Zevnik (2016). Distinguish from nihilist and lack-based notions of "nothing." Clarify the Zevnik-Prozorov debate (all vs. not-all). Draw out the first political implication (contingency of all order).

**Content**:
- 1.1 虚空不是虚无 — Three senses of "nothing" and Zevnik's affirmative third
- 1.2 虚空与第一哲学 — Return to Aristotelian first philosophy vs. "basic assumptions" in social science
- 1.3 与Prozorov的分歧：非全而非全 — The "all vs. not-all" debate and its implications for the three-layer model
- 1.4 虚空的政治含义 — Contingency of order; but also the weakness — whence legitimacy of new order?

**Sources**: Zevnik 2016 [E10] (primary), Prozorov 2014 [E7], Prozorov 2024 [N6], Molloy 2017 [N3], Özselçuk & Madra 2021 [N14] (void & formalization)

**Transition to §2**: Void tells us "it could be otherwise" but not "how otherwise." For the "how," we need the plane of immanence.

---

#### 2. 内在性平面：有结构的生成条件 (~2,200 words)

**Purpose**: Introduce the plane of immanence as the structured topological space of virtual attractors (Bonta/Protevi 2004). Explain the virtual/actual distinction as the key ontological operation. Distinguish void (nothing) from plane of immanence (not-something-but-not-nothing). Develop the political mechanisms: stratification vs. consistency, abstract machines, reciprocal causality, lines of flight and events.

**Content**:
- 2.1 从虚空到内在性平面 — The transition problem: void doesn't explain "how new emerges"
- 2.2 虚拟/现实 vs. 可能/现实 — The Deleuzian ontological operation; attractors as operational concept
- 2.3 虚空 vs. 内在性平面：最核心的区分 — The key distinction: nothing vs. structured virtual tendency
- 2.4 分层与一致性：两种模态 — Stratified (frozen/hierarchical) vs. consistent (creative/heterogeneous); the "fine file not sledgehammer" distinction; malignant vs. benign deterritorialization
- 2.5 抽象机器与互反因果性 — Abstract machines as dynamic topology; downward and upward causation
- 2.6 逃逸线与事件 — Creativity as lines of flight reaching critical thresholds; events as irreversible bifurcations

**Sources**: Bonta/Protevi 2004 [E3] (primary), Protevi 2010 [N5], DeLanda 2006 [N10], Deuchars 2010 [N1], Müller 2015 [S2], Lundy 2013 [N15]

**Transition to §3**: Both void and plane of immanence are ontological concepts — they answer "how is existence possible." But every ontological operation produces a remainder that escapes symbolization. This is the Real.

---

#### 3. 实在界：反本体论的剩余效应 (~2,000 words)

**Purpose**: Establish the Real as an anti-ontological concept, not a third ontological layer. Build the surplus model (Marx-Lacan connection via 赵淳 2024). Clarify the void-Real relationship (void as condition, Real as effect). Develop the political mechanism: interpellation's inevitable failure as the Real's political operation.

**Content**:
- 3.1 实在界不是本体论的第三层 — The Real as "gap in language" not "ultimate reality outside language"
- 3.2 "剩余"作为跨域理论模型 — The Marx-Lacan surplus model: surplus value → surplus jouissance → ideological surplus
- 3.3 虚空 vs. 实在界：一个核心公式 — Void is the ontological condition of surplus; the Real is the subjective effect of surplus
- 3.4 剩余快感的结构 — The butcher's wife story, the Tatar soldier, Ah Q drawing the circle
- 3.5 意识形态质询不完整：实在界的政治机制 — Althusser/Žižek on interpellation: the traumatic remainder that resists; the sinthome as "sacred symptom"

**Sources**: 赵淳 2024 [E12] (primary), Kinnvall/Svensson 2023 [E4], Kinnvall/Svensson 2017 [N11], Nedoh 2019 [N12], Heron 2022 [N2], Peters/Steinberg 2019 [N8] (excess as conceptual resonance)

**Transition to §4**: With all three concepts clarified, we can now integrate them into a unified three-layer model.

---

#### 4. 三层统一模型 (~1,800 words)

**Purpose**: Synthesize the three concepts into a single operational framework. Present the layered diagram. Clarify the relationships (condition → mechanism → remainder). Map each layer to a form of political action. Introduce the **Taiji analogy** as a cross-cultural conceptual resonance.

**Content**:
- 4.1 框架图 — The diagram: Void → Plane of Immanence → Existence/concretization, with the Real as surplus-effect arrow
- 4.2 三层之间的关系 — Condition (void) → mechanism (plane) → remainder (Real); not three equal layers
- 4.3 政治行动的三层次 — Each layer's "opening" corresponds to a distinct political operation: re-opening possibility (void), intervening at critical thresholds (immanence), diagnosing symptoms/remainders (Real)
- **4.4 太极类比：作为跨文化概念共振**
  - Void ≅ Taiji (太极): the undifferentiated whole, "无极而太极" (Zhou Dunyi) ≈ Zevnik's affirmative nothingness
  - Plane of Immanence ≅ Yang (阳): structured generative tendency, the pole of differentiation and actualization
  - The Real ≅ Yin (阴): remainder, resistance, the excess that every actualization leaves behind
  - Interpenetrating dots (阴中有阳, 阳中有阴): within immanence there is always a Real remainder; within the Real there is the seed of new immanence
  - **Theoretical caution**: resonance, not derivation — offered as heuristic and bridge, not as a claim about philosophical lineage
- Brief methodological note: how to operationalize the framework in empirical analysis

**Sources**: Zevnik 2016 [E10], Bonta/Protevi 2004 [E3], Prozorov 2014 [E7], Nedoh 2019 [N12], Bueger & Gadinger 2015 [S5] (practice-based IR as operationalization bridge), Zhou Dunyi *Taijitu Shuo* (太极图说) [new primary source]

**Transition to §5**: The framework's analytical purchase is tested on two empirical domains — international law and COVID-19 biopolitics.

---

#### 5. 实证锚点：三层框架的测试场 (~2,500 words)

**Purpose**: Demonstrate the framework's analytical power through two empirical tests. Structure each case as a three-layer analysis, showing how the framework can penetrate different levels of abstraction simultaneously.

**Content**:
- 5.1 克里米亚与南海：可分割学院的三个层次 (Roberts 2018)
  - Void level: contingency of legal order (same facts, opposite legal conclusions)
  - Immanence level: divided attractor basins (different legal communities, different legal trajectories)
  - Real level: the repressed return of great-power politics
- 5.2 选择性法律主义：吸引子结构的系统性重塑 (Messingschlager 2026)
  - Same three-layer structure; focus on attractor-shift (verdict economy → process outcomes)
- 5.3 COVID-19：实在界的当代回归 (Kinnvall & Svensson 2023)
  - Void level: contingency of global health order
  - Immanence level: pre-existing stratification structures (inequality, borders)
  - Real level: abjectification — whose deaths counted, whose didn't
- 5.4 COVID-19与可分割学院的交叉 — Perspective stacking: the two domains operate on different material registers (institutional vs. biological) but share the same three-layer ontological structure

**Sources**: Roberts 2018 [E8], Messingschlager 2026 [E6], Kinnvall/Svensson 2023 [E4], Kinnvall/Svensson 2017 [N11], Brannagan et al. 2026 [S6] (supplementary — additional test domain)

**Transition to §6**: The framework's greatest strength — its ability to integrate different traditions — is also its greatest vulnerability. We turn to self-critique.

---

#### 6. 临界与局限：框架的自反性 (~2,500 words)

**Purpose**: Subject the framework to its own most radical internal critique. The "cryptic rhizomatics" challenge (Bonta 2009) calls into question the emancipatory assumptions of the entire Deleuzian tradition. Five structural blind spots. Framework's self-reflexive response.

**Content**:
- 6.1 洞穴根茎：框架最锐利的自我批评 — Bonta's (2009) challenge: global corporatocracy learned every rhizome trick
- 6.2 五个结构性盲区
  - (1) Missing organizational level (Vergne 2015; Read 2008 [S7])
  - (2) Capital's "full body" requires concealment (Deleuze; MacFarlane 2017 [E5])
  - (3) Corporate ontological drift (Atal & Veldman 2025 [E1]; Veldman & Willmott 2017 [N9])
  - (4) Power forms beyond smooth/striated/holey space triad
  - (5) Concepts appropriated as surplus-extraction technologies (MacFarlane 2017; Buchanan 2015, 2017 [S3][S4])
- 6.3 框架的回应 — Not a closed system; orgology as extension, not replacement; distinguishing liberatory vs. predatory planes of immanence; maintaining reflexivity

**Sources**: Bonta 2009 [E2] (primary), MacFarlane 2017 [E5], Vergne 2015 [E9], Atal/Veldman 2025 [E1], Veldman/Willmott 2017 [N9], Lundy 2013 [N15], Buchanan 2015 [S3], Buchanan 2017 [S4], Read 2008 [S7]

**Transition to conclusion**: The framework's self-reflexive capacity is its strength, not weakness. We conclude with an application prospectus.

---

#### 结论 Conclusion (~800 words)

**Purpose**: Recapitulate the core thesis. The framework is not a "correct answer" but a coordinate system for dialogue between different political ontological traditions. Present the sea power/оceanic ontology application as a concrete research program.

**Content**:
- Summary: void opens possibility, plane of immanence provides mechanism, the Real diagnoses symptoms
- Political practice must traverse all three layers
- **Application prospectus: three-layer framework and sea power**
  - Ocean as "half-smooth space" — between territorialized land and pure void
  - Three-layer analysis of maritime disputes: void (no natural order), immanence (UNCLOS + geography as attractor basin), Real (return of excluded great-power politics)
  - The framework as conceptual creation (Deleuze: philosophy = creating concepts)

**Sources**: Steinberg/Peters 2015 [N7], Peters/Steinberg 2019 [N8], Deuchars 2010 [N1]
