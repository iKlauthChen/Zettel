### 1. No prior art on "surplus security" concept
Confirmed by prior search (2026-07-11, see `references/surplus-security-search-20260711.md`): the systematic concept of "剩余安全" (surplus security) as a theoretical construct remains novel across 4 databases, 25+ terms, and 10 DOI deep-checks.

### 2. INDOPACOM renaming/rebranding as object of analysis
No existing literature analyzes the INDOPACOM→PACOM naming cycle through a Lacanian or discourse-theoretical lens. The organizational rebranding literature does not address military command name changes. The IR strategic discourse literature treats the "Indo-Pacific" concept broadly but does not zoom into the naming act itself as a signifier cycle.
