【内容提要】安全研究长期追问"安全如何被建构"，却较少追问这一建构过程自身生产了什么。本文提出"剩余安全"概念，命名安全装置运作中持续产生的、无法被后续安全化操作吸收的结构性溢出。论证沿四个步骤展开：第一部分从唐世平"绝对安全即自我毁灭"论证与维特根斯坦遵守规则悖论出发，确立安全裂隙的本体论地位；第二部分运用拉康的对象a、幻想双重结构和享乐窃取概念，诊断剩余在安全话语中的运作机制，并整合Barkawi的安全空洞性分析与Serres的寄生物逻辑，揭示安全装置的结构性缺陷；第三部分以德勒兹编码概念为基础，区分偏执型重新领域化（P1-P5）和解域化式编码重塑（D1-D5）两种编码方向；第四部分将指标体系应用于南海COC谈判和东北亚安全架构两组案例，展示其经验可操作性。剩余安全理论将拉康派国际关系研究从"缺失范式"拓展至"剩余范式"，为理解安全实践的自我再生产机制提供了可操作的分析框架。

[English Title] Surplus Security: Fissures, Fantasy, and Coding Reshaping in Security Practice

[Abstract] Security studies has long asked "how security is constructed," but has rarely asked what the construction of security produces as its structural byproduct. This article proposes the concept of "surplus security" to name the structural overflow that security apparatuses continuously generate—an overflow that cannot be reabsorbed by subsequent securitization operations. The argument proceeds in four steps. Part I draws on Tang Shiping's thesis of "absolute security as self-destruction" and Wittgenstein's rule-following paradox to establish the ontological status of the security fissure. Part II deploys Lacan's objet a, the double structure of fantasy, and the theft of enjoyment to diagnose how surplus operates within security discourse, integrating Barkawi's analysis of security's emptiness and Serres' parasitic logic to reveal the structural deficit of the security apparatus. Part III distinguishes two coding directions based on Deleuze and Guattari: paranoid reterritorialization (P1-P5) and deterritorializing coding reshape (D1-D5). Part IV applies this indicator system to the South China Sea COC negotiations and Northeast Asian security architecture, demonstrating its empirical applicability. Surplus security theory expands Lacanian IR from the "paradigm of lack" to a "paradigm of surplus," offering an operational analytical framework for understanding the self-reproductive mechanism of security practice.

[Keywords] surplus security; security fissure; objet a; coding reshape; securitization; Lacan; Deleuze
