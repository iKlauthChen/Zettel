## 中英文摘要

### 中文摘要

**摘要**：虚空（void）、实在界（the Real）与内在性平面（plane of immanence）是当代欧陆哲学与国际关系本体论辩论中的三个核心概念，分别源自拉康派精神分析（经由Žižek的重新激活）、德勒兹哲学（经由Bonta/Protevi的复杂性科学对接）以及Zevnik对两者的整合性阅读。既有研究往往将它们视为争夺同一理论位置的竞争性概念，导致拉康派与德勒兹派之间长期的理论紧张。本文提出一个不同的解读：三者不是竞争关系，而是构成一个统一的本体论分析框架中三个不同层次的结构化回答。虚空是前本体论的肯定性"无"——一切秩序得以可能的条件。内在性平面是有结构的虚拟吸引子的拓扑空间——提供"如何不一样"的生成机制。实在界则不是独立的第三本体论层次，而是每一次本体论运作必然产生的反本体论剩余效应。本文以"剩余"概念（赵淳2024）作为连接马克思主义与精神分析的跨域理论模型，将虚空视为剩余的本体论条件，将剩余视为虚空的主体性效应。在此基础上，本文首次引入太极图（Taiji）作为跨文化概念共振——虚空对应于太极整体，内在性平面与实在界对应于阴阳鱼互含互摄。框架的分析效力通过国际法的"可分割学院"（Roberts 2017）、选择性法律主义（Messingschlager 2026）以及COVID-19疫情（Kinnvall & Svensson 2023）两个经验领域加以验证。最后，框架面对Bonta（2009）"洞穴根茎"批评的自反性反思揭示了其五个结构性盲区。本文认为，三层框架的核心贡献不在于提供一个"正确答案"，而在于为不同政治本体论传统之间的对话提供一个共同的坐标系统。

**关键词**：虚空；实在界；内在性平面；剩余；本体论；国际政治理论；太极

### English Abstract

**Abstract**: The void, the Real, and the plane of immanence are three core concepts in contemporary continental philosophy and international relations ontological debates, originating respectively from Lacanian psychoanalysis (reinvigorated by Žižek), Deleuzian philosophy (operationalized through Bonta and Protevi's engagement with complexity science), and Zevnik's integrative reading of both traditions. Existing scholarship often treats them as competing concepts vying for the same theoretical position, perpetuating the longstanding theoretical tension between Lacanian and Deleuzian schools. This article proposes an alternative reading: the three concepts are not competitors but constitute three structurally distinct layers of a unified ontological analytical framework. The void is the pre-ontological affirmative "nothing"—the condition of possibility for all orders. The plane of immanence is the structured topological space of virtual attractors—providing the generative mechanism of "how otherwise." The Real, crucially, is not a third ontological layer but the anti-ontological surplus-effect that every ontological operation necessarily produces. Drawing on Zhao Chun's (2024) "surplus" model as a cross-domain theoretical framework connecting Marx and Lacan, the article formulates the relationship as: the void is the ontological condition of surplus, and surplus is the subjective effect of the void. The article introduces, for the first time, the Taiji diagram as a cross-cultural conceptual resonance—the void corresponds to the undifferentiated whole, while the plane of immanence and the Real correspond to the interpenetrating yin-yang polarities. The framework's analytical purchase is tested on two empirical domains: the "divisible college" of international law (Roberts 2017) with selective legalism (Messingschlager 2026), and the COVID-19 pandemic's deepening of abjectification (Kinnvall & Svensson 2023). Finally, the framework confronts its own reflexive limitations through Bonta's (2009) "cryptic rhizomatics" critique, identifying five structural blind spots. The article contends that the three-layer framework's core contribution lies not in providing a "correct answer" but in offering a shared coordinate system for dialogue between different traditions of political ontology.

**Keywords**: void; the Real; plane of immanence; surplus; ontology; international political theory; Taiji
