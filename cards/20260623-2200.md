# Bonta-Protevi's core thesis: Deleuze is "the Kant of our time" — his geophilosophy provides the philosophical grounding for complexity theory just as Kant grounded classical science

## 原文要点

> "Perhaps it's not too much, then, in our view, to say that Deleuze — once his work is fully understood — can be the Kant of our time. By this we mean not the author of mediocre and racist geographical works... but the great philosopher who provided the philosophical 'grounding' of classical modern science."

> Geophilosophy is Deleuze and Guattari "linking Spinozist, Marxian, Nietzschean, and Bergsonian notions with the scientific researches known popularly as 'complexity theory'." Complexity theory investigates how systems "attain both higher levels of internal complexity and a 'focus' of systematic behaviour without having to rely on external organizing agents."

> Structure/agency dilemma: Bonta-Protevi argue DG overcome this through emergent functional structures. "Consistencies have found their 'reterritorialization' precisely in their capacity for 'deterritorialization'... they most feel at home when they are creatively changing."

> Signs as triggers: "signs are not only conceptualized as occurring beyond the register of the human and even the organic, but also are understood as triggers of material processes." The "meaning" of a sign is "a measure of the probability of triggering a particular material process."

## 我的判断

Bonta-Protevi 的问题是国内德勒兹研究中几乎完全被忽略的路径：将德勒兹-加塔利的"地缘哲学"读作复杂性理论的哲学奠基。他们的核心主张不是将 DG 应用于地理学，而是论证 DG 提供了复杂性理论所需的"形而上学"——这正是 Bergson 所说的"现代科学尚未找到的形而上学"。该书的独特价值在于：它把层次（strata）、抽象机器、一致性（consistency）、种系（phylum）等 DG 概念直接与复杂性理论的核心概念连接起来：吸引子、分岔点、突现、自组织、相变、远离平衡态、自发秩序。这与 Saldanha 的空间化进路和 Shores 的逻辑化进路完全不同：Saldanha 关注政治和物质性的空间化，Shores 关注非经典逻辑的形式化，而 Bonta-Protevi 关注自然科学哲学。三个进路各有侧重，但 Bonta-Protevi 最接近自然科学哲学，也最接近 DeLanda 的"强度科学与虚拟哲学"路线。其"结构/能动性"问题的解决策略值得注意：不是人类主体的神秘创造力，而是物质系统本身在"敏感性区域"（zones of sensitivity）中的自组织能力——这既避免了人文主义的虚构也避免了结构主义的僵化。
