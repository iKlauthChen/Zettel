# Strata are not stages but acts of capture: Deleuze-Guattari's stratoanalysis as a geological ontology of layering, coding, and territorialization

## 原文要点

> Strata are acts of capture, they are like "black holes" or occlusions striving to seize whatever comes within their reach. They operate by coding and territorialization upon the earth; they proceed simultaneously by code and by territoriality. The strata are judgments of God; stratification in general is the entire system of the judgment of God (but the earth, or the body without organs, constantly eludes that judgment, flees and becomes destratified, decoded, deterritorialized). (ATP 40)

> Stratification happens through "double articulation." A stratum consists of two pincers like a lobster. Guattari radicalizes the fourfold table (substance/form, content/expression) which Hjelmslev introduced to study the semiotic working of matter. [...] What is important is that a stratum emerges solely from the selection and condensation of particles of a different scale or order, and is immediately available for stratification on yet another level: "Each stratum serves as the substratum for another stratum" (ATP 72).

> Deleuze and Guattari make a distinction between three basic megastrata: the physicochemical, the organic, and the anthropomorphic. [...] Against theological or theosophical notions of a cosmic evolution reaching its pinnacle in man, Deleuze stresses strata are not stages. No stratum is "higher" or more perfect than another.

## 我的判断

Saldanha 对"层叠分析"（stratoanalysis）的阐释抓住了德勒兹本体论的一个关键但常被忽略的维度：层不是演化阶段（如神学宇宙论中的上升序列），也不是马克思主义的"基础-上层建筑"模式，而是"捕获行为"——通过编码（coding）和界域化（territorialization）把混沌中的流动固定下来的过程。双重分节（double articulation）的机制来自 Hjelmslev 的语言学四重表（内容的形式-内容的实体-表达的形式-表达的实体），被加塔利扩展为普适的物质-符号本体论。这意味着任何层——无论是晶体、器官、语言还是帝国——都通过两个夹钳运作：第一个在内容层面（监狱建筑 + 囚犯身体 = 监狱），第二个在表达层面（刑法话语 + 犯罪行为的陈述 = 犯罪学知识）。特别值得注意的是德勒兹和加塔利对"过于突然的解层化"的警告（ATP 503）：解层化可能导致混沌、虚空、毁灭，或更糟糕地导致法西斯主义和精神病。这不是一幅简单的"解放欲望对抗层叠"的二元图景，而是对存在之物质分层的清醒认识。
