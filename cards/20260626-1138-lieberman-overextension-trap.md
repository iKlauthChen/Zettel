# 过度延伸陷阱：威慑机动必须配套"何时停止"的脱离策略

## 原文要点

> When the state engages in a war to address its credibility problem, the state must be mindful of the fact that such wars could lead to overextension and further wars of attrition, which are the winning war strategy of NSAs. States, then, need to know when to stop and disengage. Proceeding beyond the point of initial successes leads to a greater risk of friction because capturing or holding on to territory leads to a different balance of resolve, enabling the defeated party to resort to a war of liberation and resistance.

> Frederick the Great was mindful of the issue of resolve, and he advocated quick, short wars that do not extend beyond the reach of the nation's frontiers.

> Raymond Aron advised leaders to '[g]ive voluntarily what one finally must concede' (Aron, 2002, pp. 427-28). This supports the logic of Israel's unilateral withdrawal in 2000, where, according to Aron, if a state disengages voluntarily and not under the pressure of terrorism, it will feel it has won and was not defeated. Disengagement changes the nature of the conflict from a contest of resolve back to a contest of capability, enabling an easier resolution of the credibility problem.

## 关键历史对比(1956 vs 1967):

> The victory against Egypt in 1956 led to 11 years of deterrence stability, while a more formidable defeat in 1967 led to only a few months of success. Victory leads to stability when the defender does not overextend, as was the case in the Sinai campaign in 1956, and it changes the nature of war from a contest of capability to a contest of resolve when the defender does not withdraw, as was the case after the Six Day War in 1967.

## 我的判断

这是 Lieberman 论文中理论最精妙的部分——他不只是主张"陆地机动解决威慑问题"，还揭示了机动成功的条件：**必须配套战略层面的自我约束**。1956年(胜利+不占领→11年威慑)与1967年(更辉煌的胜利+占领→仅几周威慑)的对比极具说服力。这不是"先发制人"或在威慑研究中被广泛讨论的"安全困境"，而是一个更微妙的机制：**过度延伸将战争性质从能力竞赛(capability contest)转变为意志竞赛(resolve contest)，后者正是非国家行为体的主战场**。

这为理解大国干预失败提供了一个统一框架延伸到 CB-威慑—延伸即时威慑(参见 [[刘霖炜&葛汉文 延伸—即时威慑 卡1/概念谱系|延伸-即时威慑卡组]])：不仅关乎是否介入，还关乎如何从介入中退出。

Arons 的"自愿退让"原则在理论上具有重要意义——它挑战了威慑理论中"信誉"的狭隘定义(强硬等于可信)。2000年以色列单方面撤出黎巴嫩并非"软弱"——它通过改变冲突性质(从意志竞赛回到能力竞赛)实际上增强了后续威慑效力。这一点的理论外推值得警惕但也富有启发性：它暗示**战略收缩本身可以是威慑建设的手段**，而不仅仅是对威慑失败的承认。

与 [[徐进 时间维度与战略目标 切卡|徐进的时间维度——战略目标]] 直接对话：徐进讨论的是战略目标的时间扭曲(夸大威胁/机会窗口/突发事件)，而 Lieberman 补充了另一个时间维度——**战略行动的结束节点同样是战略成败的决定性变量**。
