# Charnock's systematic exposition of Lefebvre's critical theory of space: from the production of nature (Smith/Schmidt) to the production of space (Lefebvre)

## 原文要点

> "Following Marx and Adorno, Bonefeld argues that 'the critique of political economy amounts to a critique of ontological conceptions of economic categories, including the category of labour as a transhistorically conceived category'."

> Smith: "Unless space is conceptualised as a quite separate reality from nature, the production of space is a logical corollary of the production of nature."

> "Capital produces distinct spatial scales — absolute spaces — within which the drive towards equalisation is concentrated. But it can do this only by an acute differentiation and continued redifferentiation of relative space both within and between scales."

> Charnock's argument for using Smith's production-of-nature thesis as the necessary detour to Lefebvre: Smith moves from Schmidt's critical theory of nature (socio-ecological metabolism) to the production of nature thesis, then to the production of space as its "logical corollary".

## 我的判断

Charnock 的核心策略是通过 Neil Smith 的自然生产论来引入列斐伏尔的空间生产论，把后者置于法兰克福学派批判理论的脉络中。论证链条是：马克思的"社会-生态代谢"（socio-ecological metabolism）概念 → 施密特（Alfred Schmidt）对马克思自然概念的重构 → Smith 在《不平衡发展》中从"自然生产"到"空间生产"的扩展 → 列斐伏尔的空间三维体（感知-构思-生活）。这一路径比我通常看到的直接进入《空间的生产》更准确，因为列斐伏尔的"空间"不是对"自然"的替代，而是同一"生产"逻辑在不同领域的具体化。Charnock 强调空间不是容器或空地，而是资本"按照自身形象"生产的——这意味着景观本身就是资本积累的物质化。Smith 的"均等化-差异化辩证"（dialectic of equalisation and differentiation）特别有力：资本既要扩展到一个均质化的世界市场（消除地理障碍），又要在不同尺度上制造差异（为利润梯度创造条件）。这对理解"灰色地带"等空间策略有启示。
