# 拒止-威慑悖论的制度化：以色列军事转型与内塔尼亚胡政治算计

## 原文要点

> In response to the enormous military buildup of forces, which took place after the 1973 war, leading to the 'lost decade' in the Israeli economy in the 1970s and 1980s, Israel adjusted its military strategy, placing greater emphasis on intelligence, air force, and technology. The Israeli army was molded into a small, lean, and deadly machine, which could engage and destroy its adversaries from afar. The ground forces, on the other hand, were cut back.

> Israel's strong denial capabilities, the Iron Dome missile defense system, kept Israeli casualties low, which in turn undermined its resolve to escalate and engage in a costly ground campaign of maneuver. The costs of invasion were assumed to be greater than the costs of inaction, and as Herman Kahn reminds us, offensive threats that can be destructive to oneself, lack credibility.

> Political considerations enforced deterrence dynamics. Israel's goals under the leadership of Benjamin Netanyahu were to preserve the status quo regarding the Palestinian cause and avoid having to make concessions leading to the creation of a Palestinian state. Thus, for Israel's Prime Minister Netanyahu, Hamas became an asset supported by Qatari money, because the rounds of warfare enabled him to argue that one cannot make concessions to an entity that continues to attack Israel every few years.

> When Israel became aware that Hamas' goals were not just continued rounds of warfare but a costly invasion..., Defense Minister Avigdor Lieberman advocated a preventive attack in December 2016. Israeli Prime Minister at the time, Benjamin Netanyahu, would not accept his suggestion.

## 我的判断

Lieberman 在此将微观军事因素(铁穹的技术效果)与宏观政治因素(内塔尼亚胡的分治策略)编织为一个系统性的"非决策因果链"——威慑失败不是单一决策的结果，而是在结构性条件下多年来"不做决定"(不发动机动、不预防攻击、不改变政治框架)的累积效应。

**铁穹的"成功"悖论**是 Lieberman 最有洞见的论点之一：它并非如边际主义者所主张的是"威慑的基石"(denial trumps punishment)，而是反向导致威慑失败。Herman Kahn 的引用(1962年)在此获得当代意义：如果发动攻击对自己也有破坏性，那么攻击威胁本身就是不可信的。铁穹通过降低生活成本使"不行动"的代价变得可以忍受，从而消除了解决可信性问题的政治动力——我把这个机制称为"技术性搭便车"(technological free-riding on the credibility problem)。

**内塔尼亚胡政治算计**令人联想到 [[张景全 区域化与安全化悖论 切卡|区域化与安全化悖论]]中"悖论是主动政策选择的结果"——将哈马斯维持为"资产"(而不是解决威胁)是内塔尼亚胡主动的政治选择，不是为了安全而是为了政治自保。这个悖论与 [[原始库提炼-国家·欲望·安全体制]] 中的欲望逻辑高度一致：安全体制的"成功"不在于消除威胁，而在于再生产威胁的可持续性。

2016年利伯曼防长提议预防性攻击被内塔尼亚胡否决这一具体史实尤其值得注意——它表明威慑失败不是"不可预见"的，而是在政策选择中被接受的。
