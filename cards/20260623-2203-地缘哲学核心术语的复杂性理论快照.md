# The glossary as geophilosophical tool: Bonta-Protevi's unique contribution is defining DG's spatial concepts through complexity theory — each term is given an emergent-systems reading

## 原文要点

> Abstract machine: "the most general sense of 'machine' for DG is a linkage between heterogeneous elements, so an abstract machine links virtual singularities to form multiplicities on the plane of consistency... defines the patterns and thresholds (the attractors and bifurcators, to use complexity theory terms) of a complex system."

> Assemblage: "The units of the actual are assemblages. Assemblages are composed of matters and signs. [...] They consist of bodies taken in a certain state of combination, and utterances taken in a certain regime of signs."

> Bodily without organs (ATP sense): "full BwO" allows connection with destratified bodies; "empty BwO is a black hole for subjectivity, where nothing happens." The full BwO is not anti-organs but anti-organism — "the organism is precisely a type of stratum, 'that which life sets against itself to limit itself.'"

> Refrain: DG figure of the refrain linking chaos → earth → cosmos as a diagram (see Figure 1 in book: CHAOS → unorganized milieus → territorial assemblages → plane of consistency → COSMOS).

> Deterritorialization: "intensification that produces a 'crisis' that reshuffles the 'material-semiotic' fit of an assemblage, so that 'signs' no longer have the 'meaning' they once did."

## 我的判断

这本书一半篇幅是术语表（Glossary），这本身就是一个独特的理论选择：不按线性论述而按概念群落来组织阅读，与《千高原》的"高原"形式一致。但 Bonta-Protevi 的术语表不是简单的定义汇编，而是为每个术语提供了复杂性理论的"底层映射"。例如，抽象机器被定义为连接虚拟奇异性以形成多重性的算子——也就是系统吸引子和分岔的虚拟结构。层叠（strata）是系统锁定在深吸引子盆地中的状态（强惯性模式），而一致性（consistency）是系统在远离平衡态条件下保持异质组分的状态。这个术语表的价值在于它让长期以来被神秘化的 DG 概念（如 BwO、抽象机器、重复段）获得了科学可操作性——不是还原为科学，而是展示如何用这些概念分析具体物质过程。Bonta-Protevi 图1（重复段图解：混沌→非组织环境→界域性组合→一致性平面→宇宙）是全书的一个浓缩：它展示了物质系统从混沌到自组织到界域化到虚拟平面的完整过程，这个图本身就是 DG 复杂性本体论的一个"工作模型"。
