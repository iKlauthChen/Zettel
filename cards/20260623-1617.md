# Nomads do not move: Deleuze's paradoxical redefinition of nomadism as dwelling in smooth space rather than traveling through striated space

## 原文要点

> "We can say of the nomads, following Toynbee's suggestion: they do not move. They are nomads by dint of not moving, not migrating, or holding a smooth space that they refuse to leave, that they leave only in order to conquer and die" (ATP 482).

> "To begin with, although the points determine paths, they are strictly subordinated to the paths they determine, the reverse of what happens with the sedentary. The water point is reached only in order to be left behind; every point is a relay and exists only as a relay. A path is always between two points, but the in-between has taken on all the consistency and enjoys both an autonomy and a direction of its own. The life of the nomad is the intermezzo" (ATP 380).

> "They have no history; they only have a geography" (ATP 393).

> "the nomad can be called the Deterritorialized par excellence, it is precisely because there is no reterritorialization afterward as with the migrant, or upon something else as with the sedentary (the sedentary's relation with the earth is mediatized by something else, a property regime, a State apparatus). With the nomad, on the contrary, it is deterritorialization that constitutes the relation to the earth" (ATP 381).

## 我的判断

这是全书最令人不安但也最有力的概念之一。Saldanha 对德勒兹"游牧"（nomad）的解读揭示了一个反直觉的核心论点：真正的游牧民不移动（"they do not move"）。这不是说他们静止不动，而是说他们的本质特征是"守住一个平滑空间"——他们的平滑空间不是"空旷"而是充足强度的场域，在其中路径先行于点。这完全不同于现代旅游的"点到点移动"——对一个游牧民来说，水井只是在前往下一个水井的途中被经过的中继站，而不是目的地。与此相对，现代旅行或移民从 A 到 B 移动，但移动只是达到目的的手段——"移民在到达之后重新界域化"。

这引向一个人口政治学的重要区分：游牧者不同于移民（migrants）。移民在抵达后重新界域化（附属于财产制度或国家装置），而游牧者则"在解域化本身之上再界域化"——地球自身解域化，从而为游牧民提供领土。这意味着真正的游牧生活既不是闲逛也不是逃亡，而是一种艰苦的、斯多葛式的伦理实践：面对严苛的环境（沙漠、草原、海洋、北极），不是靠寻找更适宜的地方生存，而是通过持续的身体纪律和环境嵌入来创造自己的空间。

Saldanha 也指出德勒兹游牧学的局限：它复制了"东方主义的沙漠游牧"刻板印象，对不自愿移民（难民）几乎只字未提，并且浪漫化了美国公路文学中隐藏的种族灭绝和奴隶制。这些批评是合理的但不致命——德勒兹不是在描述实际游牧民而是在创造"概念人物"（conceptual persona）。
