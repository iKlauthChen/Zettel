# The "gluon" (non-transitive identity) binds heterogeneous parts into a consistent whole without homogenizing them — Deleuze's "special logic" of fuzzy aggregates

## 原文要点

> Deleuze and Guattari: "A set is fuzzy if its elements belong to it only by virtue of specific operations of consistency and consolidation, which themselves follow a special logic." They seek what holds "heterogeneities together without their ceasing to be heterogeneous."

> Priest's gluon: "something that binds the parts into a whole." [...] The gluon is non-transitively identical with all parts: "a = g, b = g, c = g, d = g, but a ≠ b, b ≠ c, etc." The gluon of the Baked Alaska (hot meringue outside, freezing ice cream inside) is both hot and not hot (Hg ∧ ¬Hg) — a dialetheic entity.

> "The intercalary elements that make the bonds precarious suggest that there is a factor that makes the parts not apt to combine or stay bonded, despite the fact that they do at some moment."

## 我的判断

Shores 第二章是全书最精彩的部分之一。他追踪了德勒兹与加塔利的"一致性"（consistency）概念从 Eugène Dupréel 的"布丁石"（puddingstone——由大小不一的卵石胶结而成的沉积岩）的来源，然后论证这一异质性组合的逻辑既不是经典逻辑的排他性构成，也不是模糊逻辑的部分真值，而是 Priest 的"gluon"——一个非传递性同一的 dialetheic 实体。关键洞察在于，布丁石的卵石（异质部分）通过胶结物被"粘合"在一起，但胶结物（gluon）与每个卵石同一，而卵石之间却不相同——这就是非传递性同一。当这个模型应用到时间过程（如 Alice 从小变大的不同阶段）时，时间性 gluon 将不同阶段"绑"在一起，但不同阶段的自身却不被还原为同一，从而既保持变化的一致性又不丧失异质性。这为德勒兹的"异质性组合"（heterogeneous composition）提供了一种精确的逻辑表述。对我来说，这里浮现了"剩余安全"的一个可能形式化路径：安全不是一个同质状态，而是异质要素通过一种非传递性"胶合"维持在一起的临时一致性——这种胶合本身是矛盾的（既安全又不安全）。
