# The politics of orientation: under neoliberal functional differentiation, democratic politics shifts from effective steering to providing complexity-reducing orientation — and post-truth populism thrives as its most efficient form

## 原文要点

> Richter's definition of orientation: "a form of democratic politics in which authority and legitimacy rest not on whether and how political actors shape or propose to shape society through effective decision making but on the means of 'contingency control' and 'uncertainty absorption' they offer the citizens of complex, deterritorialized democracies."

> "Populism, post-truth politics, and conspiracy theories thrive under these conditions because they are particularly effective in offering complexity-reducing orientation for sense-making."

> The shift to a politics of orientation "benefits those political forces whose simplistic messages offer the most radical complexity-reduction and thus the most effective orientation — which are, as it stands, the political voices of the populist right."

> However, Richter insists: "While there is no easy way out of a democratic politics that social conditions have geared toward the provision of orientation, such a politics is not exhausted in the post-truth populism that currently shapes its appearance."

## 我的判断

Richter 的核心理论贡献在第五章和第六章完全展开。"取向政治"（politics of orientation）概念极有新意：它不是把当代民主危机解释为制度的衰落或民粹主义的入侵，而是理解为民主政治对新自由主义条件的一种功能性适应。当资本和数字化使社会变得极度复杂、政治系统无法有效理解（更不用说控制）经济、法律、生态等领域时，政治就不能再声称它能解决社会问题——它转向提供"复杂性减缩"：问题诊断、价值观体系、叙事、解释框架。后真相民粹主义正是这种取向政治最有效的表现形式：它的信息极其简化，提供的"意义缩减"最彻底，因此最能满足迷失的公民的取向需求。重要的是，Richter 坚持这不是简单的衰落叙事——取向政治不仅是民粹主义，"想象一种不同的民主取向政治"是本书留给读者的开放性挑战。Richter 的分析路径——不是道德谴责民粹主义，而是揭示其运作的社会-功能性条件——对我的"剩余安全"理论有直接启发：安全也应当被理解为在复杂性条件下提供取向的功能，而非消除威胁的实际能力。
