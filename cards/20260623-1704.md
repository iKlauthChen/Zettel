# Incompossibility and Stoic co-fatality introduce a bifurcational, non-chronological time-structure into Deleuze's logic — the strongest case for dialetheism

## 原文要点

> For Leibniz, "there are many possible biblical Adams, with each having its own unique set of predicates." Adam the non-sinner is "possible given that his notion involves no contradiction," but he is "not possible in combination with the other individual substances in our world." Deleuze's removal of God from this picture: incompossibility "no longer serves to exclude events from one another by means of a negative use of divergence of disjunction."

> Stoic moral choice "introduces divergence into the course of time on both the aiônian and chronosian levels." The Aion time of the event is not the Chronos time of bodies — the Stoic "prohairesis" (moral choice) can "disjoin the course of time, like changing the trajectory of an arrow while it is already moving."

> The Garden of Forking Paths (Borges): "He chooses — simultaneously — all of them. He creates, in this way, diverse futures, diverse times which themselves also proliferate and fork."

## 我的判断

第五章对德勒兹的不共可能性（incompossibility）理论和斯多葛命运学说进行了细致梳理，揭示了德勒兹时间哲学中的"分叉"（bifurcation）结构。对于莱布尼茨，不共可能性是通过"排除析取"来运作的：既然上帝选择了最佳世界，Adam-罪人与Adam-非罪人就是不共可能的，只能取其一。德勒兹从莱布尼茨体系中抽掉上帝后，世界的分叉就不再是被预先排除的，而是真实共存但又互不兼容的——这便是"肯定性综合析取"的时间性含义。Shores 认为这些同时存在的但互不兼容的分叉构成了一种 dialetheic 时间：在分叉点上，世界既走这条路径又走那条路径。更有趣的是斯多葛学派的 co-fatality 概念——在严格的宿命论下，人的道德选择（prohairesis）仍然可以在时间中引入新的方向，但这并不违反命运，因为这种"偏离"本身也是命运的一部分。斯多葛的 Aion（事件/命运的无限时间）和 Chronos（身体/顺序的时间）之间的区分在这里至关重要。这对我有直接启发：剩余安全不是在 Chronos 中预先决定的、可预期的状态，而是在 Aion 中通过事件"分叉"涌现出来的、与不可预测的命运"合作"的产物。
