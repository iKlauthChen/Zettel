# Zevnik's core thesis: developing a monist ontology of the "not-all" (pas-tout) by folding Lacan and Deleuze to rethink the political subject beyond Cartesian dualism

## 原文要点

> "This intervention is based on the assumption that modern political categories are a heritage of a long history of western philosophy and metaphysics. Thus, the exclusions, inequality... are not only consequential to political will but result from the legacy of how we think and practise politics."

> "I take a monist-oriented ontology of the not-all as such first philosophy." The void or nothingness is not nihilistic but "implies that there is nothing in the 'image' itself that facilitates and conditions a particular understanding or comprehension of it."

> Distinction between being and existence: "being is an immanent plane, a reservoir of force facilitating the possibility of existence, then existence is a transcendent embodiment of being (its appearance in the world). It is not that being allows for only one account of existence... instead there are many ways in which being can be embodied, the subject merely results from a particular ordering logic 'imposed' on the process of being's transformation into existence."

> Zevnik follows Negri's "common" rather than Nancy's "humanity" — the common as "a potentiality, or a time when something special can occur."

## 我的判断

Zevnik 最核心的理论创新是提出"非全"（not-all / pas-tout）的一元论本体论，区别于 Prozorov 的"虚空普遍主义"（void universalism）的全（all）。她认为本体论意义上的最终支撑是"空"（nothing/void），但这不是"全"的空（如巴迪乌-普罗佐洛夫的集合论公理），而是"非全"的空——总有些东西尚未进入符号化、尚未被驯化。这直接来自拉康晚年关于女性享受（feminine jouissance）和"非全"（pas-tout）的学说，以及德勒兹的单一性（univocity）理论。这一区分的政治含义是深远的：如果"空"是全的，那么政治斗争就只是等待一个事件打破既有的符号秩序；如果"空"是非全的，那么政治斗争就是持续开放的——总有未被捕捉到的剩余，总有潜在的存在形式尚未涌现。Zevnik 的"存在"（being）vs "生存"（existence）区分也很关键：存在是内在的力的平面（Spinoza 的 conatus），生存是存在在世界的超越性具身——主体的形式只是众多生存形式中的一种，是特定的"秩序逻辑"（law/language）施加于存在-转化为-生存过程的结果。这为"非主体性的政治存在"——如关塔那摩被拘留者的成为-动物、成为-被拘留者——提供了本体论基础。
