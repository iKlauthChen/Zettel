# Deleuze's ontology (virtual/actual, event, becoming, intensity) mapped onto complexity theory's key concepts: attractors, bifurcators, self-organizing criticality, emergent structures

## 原文要点

> "Deleuze always avoided [the closure of metaphysics], insisting 'I've never been worried about going beyond metaphysics or any death of philosophy.' Instead of denying the possibility of a new metaphysics, Deleuze tried to construct one."

> Virtual vs actual: "The basic Deleuzean distinction set forth in DR between virtual and actual enables us to account for unpredictability in physical systems while still maintaining a consistent materialism. Attractors are structures for the self-organization of processes in which physical systems of matter-energy flow can become organized."

> Two sources of creativity in material systems: (1) "'Zones of sensitivity'... when sensitive systems pick up slight cues that trigger a bifurcation"; (2) "When two or more material systems, with their attendant virtuals, come together in a 'becoming' that provides an unpredictably new 'Idea', a new state space erupts."

> The event: "What Deleuze in the 1960s calls 'Events' (and later with Guattari will call 'lines of flight') are changes brought about unpredictably when sensitive systems pick up slight cues that trigger a bifurcation."

## 我的判断

Bonta-Protevi 最具原创性的贡献在于系统性地将德勒兹的核心本体论概念翻译为复杂性理论的术语。这不是应用或类比，而是主张它们描述的是同一个物质世界——德勒兹提供的是复杂性理论所需要的"形而上学"（metaphysics），正如康德为牛顿物理提供了形而上学基础。关键映射关系如下：

- **虚拟（virtual）** ↦ 吸引子结构（attractor landscape）
- **实际化（actualization）** ↦ 自组织过程 
- **事件（event）** ↦ 分岔点（bifurcation point）
- **生成（becoming）** ↦ 两个系统的耦合创造新的状态空间
- **强度（intensity）** ↦ 临界状态（criticality）/系统敏感性
- **抽象机器（abstract machine）** ↦ 准因果算子连接虚拟多重性
- **一致性（consistency）** ↦ 远离平衡态的自组织

这个映射的风险在于可能把德勒兹还原为复杂性理论的通俗化版本。Bonta-Protevi 的回应是：他们的德勒兹是"政治性地运用"复杂性理论——社会系统不仅仅是自组织的，而且充满了权力、控制和解放的可能性。政治介入（战争机器、解域化、游牧）不是外在于系统的，而是复杂性——临界状态、分岔、涌现——的必然延伸。这个立场使其区别于纯科学的 DeLanda 路线，也比 Saldanha 更靠近自然科学。对我而言，这一框架可以直接移植到"剩余安全"的复杂性理论化：安全不是系统的稳定状态（深度吸引子），而是系统在临界状态下分岔时涌现的临时性"一致性"——既不能还原为底层组件也不能被自上而下控制。
