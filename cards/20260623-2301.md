# Žižek's Hegel: the negation of negation does not return to positivity — it is the redoubling of lack, "negation without a filling," the movement "from nothing to nothing to nothing"

## 原文要点

> Žižek's formula for Hegelian negativity: "The transition, or becoming, sublates itself in its passage; the other that in this transition comes to be, is not the non-being of a being, but the nothingness of a nothing, and this, to be the negation of a nothing, constitutes being. Being only is as the movement of nothing to nothing, and as such it is essence; and the latter does not have this movement within it, but is this movement as a being that is itself absolutely illusory, pure negativity, outside of which there is nothing for it to negate but which negates only its own negative, and this negative, which latter is only in this negating."

> Hegel vs Hölderlin: "Hegel occupies here a fourth position — what he adds to Hölderlin is a purely formal shift of transposing the tragic gap that separates the reflecting subject from pre-reflexive Being into this Being itself. Once we do this, the problem becomes its own solution: it is our very division from absolute Being which unites us with it, since this division is immanent to Being."

> "The answer to 'Why is there Something rather than Nothing?' is thus that there is only Nothing, and all processes take place 'from Nothing through Nothing to Nothing.' However, this nothing is not the Oriental or mystical Void of eternal peace, but the nothingness of a pure gap (antagonism, tension, 'contradiction'), the pure form of dislocation ontologically preceding any dislocated content."

> Parataxis: "In praise of Understanding" — Hegel's "Understanding" (Verstand) is not merely overcome by Reason (Vernunft) but persists as the "torture-house of language" that perpetually destabilizes identity.

## 我的判断

Žižek 对黑格尔的解读在全书中具有双重功能：既是重建被主流哲学（分析传统、后结构主义）歪曲的黑格尔，也是为拉康的精神分析提供一个黑格尔式的本体论基础。核心论证集中在"否定之否定"（negation of the negation）的含义上。通常的解读（从马克思到萨特）认为否定之否定恢复肯定——第一否定取消 A，第二否定取消否定并回到 A+（更高层次的肯定）。Žižek 论证这完全是误解：否定之否定不是"双重否定=肯定"，而是"缺失的自我加倍"——第一次否定产生缺失，第二次否定否定的是缺失本身，但这不是恢复充实而是把缺失转移到新的层次。这就是"从无到无到无"的运动：存在本身只是这个运动的纯粹形式。

Žižek 通过 Hölderlin 的"偏心率路径"（eccentric path）来展示黑格尔的独特性：Hölderlin 认为主体分裂于存在是不可克服的悲剧，只能通过叙事来"和解"；黑格尔则把分裂本身转移到存在之中——不是我们无法到达存在，而是存在本身已经分裂。这就是"实体即主体"（Not only as Substance, but also as Subject）的含义：实体不是静态的底基，而是自我否定的运动。Žižek 进而讨论"并列"（parataxis）——黑格尔的"知性"（Understanding/Verstand）不是被理性（Reason/Vernunft）简单消除，而是持存为"语言的酷刑室"——知性的固定区分不断被辩证运动拆解但又是辩证运动的前提。这个"知性的赞美"（In praise of Understanding）是对德勒兹-加塔利"否定不是肯定的"的正面回应：正是知性的钉子（即那个不断被排斥的骨头）使辩证运动保持活力。
