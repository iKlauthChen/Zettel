# Critical systems theory: folding Deleuze and Luhmann produces a postfoundational critique distinct from the Frankfurt School — critique without normative certainty

## 原文要点

> Richter: "Luhmann's rejection of the normative certainty underpinning Frankfurt School critical theory echoes criticisms put forward by poststructuralist thinkers." Luhmann's theory "embraces contingency and ontological insecurity to capture, and retain, an open-ended potentiality that safeguards against political oppressiveness."

> Esposito on Luhmann: "Critical observation, which looks for the conditions that make these improbabilities normal [...] is observation of society within society. From this perspective, sociological systems theory could be seen somehow provocatively as the most accomplished form of the critical attitude — a reflexive form of critique."

> Postfoundationalism (Marchart): "the primordial (or ontological) absence of an ultimate ground is itself the condition of possibility of grounds as present."

> Richter's three characteristics of the Deleuze in this book: (1) Deleuze as political theorist, not just ontologist with political implications; (2) Deleuze as analyst of structural continuity as well as revolutionary change; (3) Deleuze as surface philosopher (Nietzsche, sense, event) rather than materialist depth (Bergson, Spinoza).

## 我的判断

Richter 在导论中明确定位自己的项目为"批评的系统理论"（critical systems theory），试图从句法兰克福学派中把卢曼的"飞行物体"带回到地面。这一理论姿态包含了双重拒绝：既拒绝法兰克福学派的规范性确定性（Habermas 的沟通理性），也拒绝卢曼技治主义解读的社会功能主义。Richter 认为卢曼提供了法兰克福学派之外的一种批判形式——不是通过诉诸超越的规范性标准来批判社会，而是通过揭示社会秩序的"不可可能性"（improbability）来运作。Esposito 的表述尤其到位：批判性观察寻找那些使得"本来极不可能的事看起来正常"的条件。Richter 对 Deleuze 的界定也值得注意——她选择了一个"表面哲学家"的德勒兹（Nietzsche、意义、事件），而非"深层物质"的德勒兹（Bergson、Spinoza、虚拟/强度）。这种选择与 Saldanha（物质性/地理）和 Shores（逻辑形式化）形成鲜明对比。这提示我：同一个德勒兹因不同的折叠对象而产生完全不同的理论产出——与地理学折叠产生空间理论，与逻辑学折叠产生 dialetheism，与系统论折叠产生取向政治。
