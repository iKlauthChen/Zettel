# The plateau and the egg: two complementary figures of intensive spatiotemporal dynamism — one for writing and social organization, one for biological individuation

## 原文要点

> "We call a 'plateau' any multiplicity connected to other multiplicities by superficial underground stems in such a way as to form or extend a rhizome" (ATP 22). A plateau is not a chapter but a plane of intensity held in continuous variation without climax — borrowed from Bateson's description of Balinese sexual and aggressive play as self-correcting feedback loops.

> "The entire world is an egg" (DR 216). "Embryology already displays the truth that there are systematic vital movements, torsions and drifts, that only the embryo can sustain: an adult would be torn apart by them" (DR 118).

> "Intensity is implicated in itself, while it is cancelled by being explicated in extensity" (DR 228). There are four figures of folding: perplication (mutual entanglement of Ideas), complication (system's complexity), implication (movement from intensive to extensive), explication (actual system with identifiable extensive characteristics).

## 我的判断

高原（plateau）和卵（egg）是德勒兹空间性的两个互补形象——一个针对文本和社会的组织方式（横向连接、非等级、强度维持在顶点以下），一个针对个体化和形态发生（从虚拟的强度场实际化为分化的有机体）。高原来自贝特森（Bateson）对巴厘文化中高潮回避行为的观察——在游戏、性爱和仪式中，参与者维持在一个高度激发但不走向高潮的强度平台上。德勒兹和加塔利把这个概念从人类学转借到哲学写作中：《千高原》本身就是一个高原网络——一个章节不是从开始到结束的线性论证，而是一个强度平面，在其中读者可以任意进入和退出。卵则是强度场如何在空间中分化的模型：胚胎发育的所有关键事件——轴线的建立、细胞层的折叠、区域位移——都是空间-时间动力机制（spatiotemporal dynamisms）的表现。德勒兹的"整个宇宙是卵"意味着一切实体都是从这样的强度场中"展开"出来的。

但 Saldanha 指出一个重要局限：德勒兹对卵的运用可能趋向于一种准生机论——仿佛一切系统都"像"胚胎发育。他正确地提醒我们，生态群体的运动分布、资本的流动、城市的增长——这些都是不同种类的差异，不能用同一"卵模型"还原。
