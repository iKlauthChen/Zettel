# Richter's core thesis: folding Deleuze and Luhmann through the hinge of "sense" (Sinn) to develop a political theory of orientation for 21st-century democracy

## 原文要点

> This book employs the theories of Deleuze and Luhmann to develop a political theory of twenty-first-century democratic politics. [...] The arguments developed in this book will unfold from a journey into the hinterland of Luhmann's thought. [...] Folding aims at neither comparison nor synthesis but rather at the creative genesis of a theoretical third no longer purely Deleuzian or Luhmannian.

> Under conditions of neoliberal capitalism, which the "post-mortem despotism" of liberal democracy can no longer effectively control, it becomes a politics of orientation that continues modern politics with different means. [...] A politics of orientation sustains its position as society's steering authority by guiding subjects on how to make sense of this world, and of their own position within it, by offering problem diagnoses, value systems, narratives, and explanatory frameworks.

## 我的判断

Richter 将两个看似不可通约的理论——德勒兹的后结构主义本体论与卢曼的社会系统论——通过"意义/感知"（sense/Sinn）这一枢纽进行"折叠"（folding），产生了一个全新的政治理论视角。这一方法论本身（非比较非综合，而是创造性的理论第三项）对跨学科理论构建非常有启发性。核心切入点是：卢曼在其《社会理论》第一卷中引用了德勒兹的《意义的逻辑》，这意味着卢曼注意到了二人意义理论的深层共鸣。对德勒兹和卢曼来说，意义都是彻底内在的——没有外在根基、没有终极起源，总是在意义关系内部不断自我生产的。这种内在性创造通过"无意义/噪杂"（nonsense/noise）的构成性外部驱动：每一次意义表达都排除了一个外部，但这个外部本身是意义表达的内在产物。Richter 的原创性贡献在于把这种认识论/本体论框架政治化：当新自由主义资本主义使民主政治的"直接导向"（steering）能力失效后，政治的功能从"做出集体约束性决策"转向"提供复杂性减缩的取向"（orientation）。这是"后真相政治"和民粹主义繁荣的结构性条件。
