# Capital as axiomatic: Deleuze-Guattari's political economy centers on money's deterritorializing force, not class struggle

## 原文要点

> Capitalism is in fact born of the encounter of two sorts of flows: the decoded flow of production in the form of money-capital, and the decoded flow of labor in the form of the "free worker." Hence, unlike previous social machines, the capitalist machine is incapable of providing a code that will apply to the whole of the social field. By substituting money for the very notion of a code, it has created an axiomatic of abstract quantities that keeps moving further and further in the direction of the deterritorialization of the socius. (AO 33)

> "If capitalism is the exterior limit of all societies, this is because capitalism for its part has no exterior limit, but only an interior limit that is capital itself and that it does not encounter, but reproduces by always displacing it" (AO 230-31).

> "It is at the level of flows, the monetary flows included, and not at the level of ideology, that the integration of desire is achieved" (AO 239).

## 我的判断

Saldanha 正确地将德勒兹-加塔利的政治经济学定位于马克思-布罗代尔-阿明的世界体系理论传统中，但突出了两个独特贡献。第一，对德勒兹和加塔利而言，资本不是对劳动的剥削关系（马克思的黑格尔残留），而是货币流与劳动力流的"衔接"（conjugation）——资本诞生于这两种被解码了的流的相遇。这意味着解域化先于阶级关系。第二，"公理性"（axiomatic）概念：资本的运作不依赖于意识形态的合法性（如阿尔都塞），而直接通过货币量的抽象等价性运作于欲望层面。这意味着意识形态不再是歪曲的意识，而是对累积可能性的有意识拥抱。工具和身体被"机械奴役"（machinic enslavement）直接插入资本流，而不是通过虚假意识的中介。这对空间理论至关重要：资本通过解域化来重塑地球表面，但这种解域化总是伴随再界域化——全球化不是同质化的过程，而是梯度（gradients）和不均衡（unevenness）的生产。精神分裂（schizophrenia）在这里被德勒兹和加塔利从精神病理学"空运"到资本主义的普遍状态：当所有编码断裂，谵妄和幻觉就接管了社会场域。
