# Smooth vs striated space: Deleuze's fundamental topological distinction between nomadic and sedentary spatial logics, with the caveat that neither is inherently good

## 原文要点

> "Never believe that a smooth space will suffice to save us" (ATP 500). If it is true that Deleuze and Guattari throughout their work defend the smooth against the striated, this is to counter the philosophical laziness of the hegemonic Western approaches to space as orthogonal and homogenous. Real space is "holey" in that its striations always harbor gaps through which escape becomes possible (ATP 413-15).

> The smooth/striated plateau presents several more "models" of the two kinds of space that amount to a kind of methodology as well as politics. First, textiles: weaving vs felt. Second, music. Third, navigation. Fourth, mathematics (Riemannian manifolds vs Euclidian geometry). Fifth, physics (thermodynamics and chaos theory vs classical mechanics). The last model is art history (nomad art vs imperial art).

> Under neoliberal capitalism almost everything creates value: "capitalism operates less on a quantity of labor than by a complex qualitative process bringing into play modes of transportation, urban models, the media, the entertainment industries, ways of perceiving and feeling — every semiotic system" (ATP 492).

## 我的判断

平滑空间（smooth space）与条纹化空间（striated space）是德勒兹空间哲学中最广为人知的概念对。Saldanha 的叙述有三大优点。一是他展示了这个区分的五个模型——纺织、音乐、航海、数学、物理学、艺术史——并强调这些模型之间不是类比关系而是"共振"（resonance）：黎曼流形与海洋的平滑性是同一抽象机器在不同领域的表现。二是他明确指出平滑并不是好的、条纹化并不是坏的——资本的运作恰恰是通过平滑空间（旅游、金融流、信息高速公路）来实现其控制。US 城市是笛卡尔条纹化的顶点，但同时也即时地向金融区的平滑空间和逃避郊区的嬉皮士开放。三是"洞状空间"（holey space）的概念——条纹化总是包含缝隙，逃逸始终可能。我注意到 Saldanha 在地缘政治的语境中隐含了一个批评：如果平滑空间和条纹化空间不是规范性的对立，那么德勒兹式政治的伦理核心是什么？他的回答似乎是"战斗"和"残酷"——在平滑与条纹之间的张力中生活，既不把条纹化视为必然也不把平滑视为救赎。
