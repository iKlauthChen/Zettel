# Deleuze's "affirmative synthetic disjunction" is not classical inclusive disjunction but a non-exclusive otherthanness consistent with dialetheic negation

## 原文要点

> Routley and Routley on negation: classical negation yields "exclusive and exhaustive" otherthanness — from A ∧ ¬A, everything explodes. The third model of negation yields "non-exclusive restricted otherthanness": "A and ¬A are suitably independent though nonetheless related; A and ¬A may both fail together and differently may both hold together; A and ¬A neither cancel nor implode one another."

> Deleuze and Guattari on Klossowski: "In Le Baphomet Klossowski contrasts God as the master of the exclusions and restrictions that derive from the disjunctive syllogism, with an antichrist who is the prince of modifications, determining instead the passage of a subject through all possible predicates. I am God I am not God, I am God I am Man: it is not a matter of a synthesis that would go beyond the negative disjunctions [...] but rather of an inclusive disjunction that carries out the synthesis itself in drifting from one term to another and following the distance between the terms."

## 我的判断

第四章处理德勒兹对否定和析取的态度的逻辑基础。Shores 巧妙论证了德勒兹拒绝的"否定"是经典排斥性否定（A ∧ ¬A → 爆炸），而不是 paraconsistent/dialetheic 的"非排他性限制性他性"（non-exclusive restricted otherthanness）。这意味着德勒兹的"肯定性综合析取"（affirmative synthetic disjunction）——他声称不是否定而是肯定的容纳——实际上可以用 dialetheic 的 A ∧ ¬A 来形式化，前提是用 paraconsistent 否定替代经典否定。Klossowski 的 Baphomet（反基督）形象在这里成为关键：Baphomet 是"修改之王"——不是通过排除一个选项来肯定另一个（God 的逻辑），而是让主体通过所有可能的谓词，"我是上帝我不是上帝，我是上帝我是人"。Shores 认为这种"包容性析取"在经典逻辑中不存在对应物，但 dialetheic 逻辑的 A ∧ ¬A 具有相同的逻辑特征：它保持异质性、肯定两个项和它们之间的差异、不把项还原为同一。这个论证在逻辑技术层面很有说服力，但仍然留下了德勒兹自己的"肯定"概念与 dialetheic 的"矛盾"概念之间的张力——德勒兹拒绝"否定"的修辞确实与 dialetheic 的方案存在表面冲突，Shores 解决这一问题的方式是将这种冲突归咎于德勒兹对非古典逻辑的不熟悉。
