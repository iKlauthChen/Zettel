# The bifurcating event is the source of both political continuity and change — modern democratic politics functions as the authoritative decision on which pathway of sense is actualized

## 原文要点

> Richter on the transition from ontology to political theory: "In the context of society's sense-relations, the decision on this actualization marks the function of modern politics." The event simultaneously ruptures and continues temporal ordering in sense — it is a "bifurcation point" where multiple possible world-lines coexist.

> Drawing on Deleuze's reading of Leibniz (incompossible worlds) and Prigogine/Stengers (bifurcation points), Richter argues that political events are moments when the chaotic multiplicity of sense is selectively condensed into a particular world. The political decision is never merely reactive but creatively productive of the social reality it claims to represent.

> Luhmann's political system is autopoietically closed — it can only make sense of its environment (economy, law, ecology) from within its own political logic. Real steering is impossible; politics can only produce "collectively binding decisions" that reduce complexity for the rest of society.

## 我的判断

第四章是全书从本体论到政治理论的转折点。Richter 通过"事件"（event）——德勒兹的"纯粹事件"与卢曼的"系统扰动"的折叠——来解释政治变化如何可能。关键论点是：事件不是在新自由主义条件下消失了（如"再也没有事件"式的末世论），而是在"分叉点"上同时开辟多条可能的路径，而政治的功能就是在这些路径中做出权威性的选择决策。但这也是全书论证中最有张力的环节：如果社会系统是自创生的（autopoietic），对它的环境只有有限的理解能力，那么政治决策就不可能真正"导向"社会——它只能做出"集体约束性决策"来吸收不确定性，而不能真正解决问题。Richter 正确地将卢曼的政治系统描述为一种"后现代君主"——它声称是社会的导向中心，但实际上只是社会众多意义系统中的一个，对经济、法律、生态等领域的理解极其有限。这一诊断的一个没有完全展开的含义是：如果政治决策本质上是盲目的，那么民主问责制度——基于公民评价政策效果的制度——的基础就受到了根本质疑。
