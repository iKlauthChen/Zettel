# The autopoietic monad: Deleuze's reading of Leibniz (the fold) and Luhmann's systems theory converge on a model of windowless self/world co-production through sense

## 原文要点

> Luhmann's system is an "autopoietic monad" — like Leibniz's monad, it has "neither doors nor windows, where all activity takes place on the inside." But autopoiesis does not equate to isolation: systems require "building material" from outside. "Systems must be informationally open to be able to operate as functionally closed."

> Deleuze on the fold: "the soul has folds and is full of folds." The creative fold at the monadic surface can enfold material singularities to charge self- and worldmaking. But any attribution of the singularities enfolded to either material outside or representational inside is "secondary to the worldmaking process that is being charged."

> Perspectivism is a pluralism (Deleuze): without Leibniz's divine harmony guaranteeing the "best of all possible worlds," the monadic multiverse becomes a chaosmos. "Multiple windowless selves inhabit multiple worlds with no ground to ensure their cohesion and semblance."

## 我的判断

Richter 的第二章借用莱布尼茨-德勒兹的"折叠"（fold）和单子论来强化卢曼的自创生系统论，论证自我和世界在同一意义创造过程中共同生产。这个论证的关键力量在于：它否定了任何外部根基的可能性——无论是德勒兹式的"虚拟"（作为深层本体论来源）还是康德式的先验条件。Richter 通过 Zourabichvili 的"德勒兹没有本体论"论题来强化自己的立场：如果一切都发生在意义的表面，那么所谓深层本体论只是表面意义的另一种表达。但她没有完全解决一个内在张力：卢曼的自创生系统虽然声称没有外部根基，但其功能分化的社会系统似乎非常稳定和封闭——如果一切都可以不同，为什么实际的变化如此困难？Richter 试图通过卢曼自己对史前社会（非功能分化但仍然是意义系统）的分析来论证"功能分化是偶然历史产物"，这比为大多数卢曼主义者的立场——功能性分化是现代性的不可逆特征——提供了更大的开放性。但这一论证仍然停留在原则层面，没有具体说明如何从功能分化社会"逃逸"出去。
