# The danger of a new spatial fetish: Unwin argues that the fetishization of "production of space" leads to an arid conceptualization divorced from empirical reality

## 原文要点

> Unwin on the risk of spatial fetishism: "Is there not therefore a danger that we are moving towards a new kind of spatial fetish, albeit of a different form to that which emerged in geography during the 1950s and 1960s?"

> On empirical application: "If our task is to study the production of space, what exactly is it that we 'must' study? Take, for example, the question of political violence... state formation... ethnic cleansing... massacres in Rwanda and Burundi..."

> On Harvey's grid: "By giving concrete expression to Lefebvre's essentially fluid ideas, Harvey effectively destroys their enigmatic and uncertain character. Lefebvre's triad is continually in motion, and by trying to tie it down... Harvey departs considerably from Lefebvre's original intentions."

> On meaning: "Lefebvre makes the concept lose meaning; his all-inclusiveness takes meaning away from any definition that he attributes to space."

## 我的判断

Unwin 对"新空间拜物教"的警告特别重要。他指出 1950-60 年代的计量革命曾把空间当作一个独立的、客观的维度来研究（所谓"空间科学"），而 Lefebvre-Harvey-Soja 的传统坚决反对这种空间概念。但 Unwin 认为他们正在制造一种相反但同样有问题的拜物教——不是把空间当作独立容器，而是把"空间生产"当作一切社会过程的核心以至排除了其他分析维度。也就是说，"空间转向"从纠正一种简化走向了自己特有的简化。对 Harvey 的"空间实践网格"的批评也很犀利：Lefebvre 的三元组本质上是流动的、非确定性的，而 Harvey 把它固定在四个维度的网格中，实际上破坏了其内在活力和不确定性。Unwin 关于"我们应该研究什么"的实践问题——例如种族清洗中真正重要的是"空间的生产"还是"人类苦难的生产条件？"——暴露了这一理论的实证应用困境：框架本身没有提供研究方法论。这对我思考如何"应用"德勒兹空间哲学（以及列斐伏尔空间批判）有警示：必须避免一种新的概念拜物教，确保理论工具不遮蔽而非阐明分析对象。
