# 陆地机动解决了累积威慑无法解决的"可信性问题"

## 原文要点

> The credibility problem is the crux of the deterrence problem and... its resolution cannot be obtained through coercion or the use of cumulative deterrence, in the absence of a prior military victory. ... the Sinai campaign of 1956 made it happen and suggests the need to examine which one of the causal mechanisms that theoretically lead to the resolution of the credibility problem is more important: coercion or land maneuver.

> Thomas Schelling makes a similar observation about the credibility of coercion before and after defeat in war. Schelling argued that credibility must be demonstrated. Coercion, according to him, occurs after the use of brute force and defeat (Schelling, 1966, pp. 1-34). War is thus logically within the scope of deterrence theory because it serves an important integral function to deterrence—it solves the credibility problem.

> According to James Fearon, even if the defender's costly signals to resist with force might be credible during the crisis phase of the interaction, they are least likely to have an effect. In his analysis of the interaction between general and immediate deterrence, Fearon argues that a failure of general deterrence suggests that the challenger is highly motivated and is willing to assume the risks of the challenge even if the defender's threat is credible.

> Land maneuver forces the terrorist organization out of its hiding places to confront the state's power. Identifying the terrorist organization's critical centers of gravity and attacking them with massive ground forces would overwhelm the organization and force it to lose many of an NSA's advantages, such as tactics of evasion and disappearance from the battlefield by embedding itself within the civilian population. By forcing the terrorist organization to fight a ground war, the state can impose its logic of the war on the contest and win it.

## 我的判断

Lieberman 拒绝了一个广泛接受的理论前提即"累积威慑(Tit-for-Tat + 惩罚 + 拒止)的递进使用最终会转化为战略威慑稳定"。他借 Fearon 的"选择效应"论证指出：**一旦一般威慑已经失败(表明挑战者高度有动机)，危机阶段的昂贵信号恰恰是最不可能有效的**。这颠倒了边际主义者的因果链。

他进一步提出陆地机动的两重机制：(1) **物质层面**——迫使非国家行为体从隐匿中现身，丧失其不对称优势；(2) **主体间层面**——通过"示范"击败而非"告知"成本，重建国家使用武力的可信性。这与 Schelling 在 Arms and Influence 中提出的"先有 brute force 后有 coercion"的层级结构一致。

关键是 Lieberman 明确区分了"costly signal"(危机阶段)和"decisive victory"(战争结果)——大多数威慑文献将两者混为一谈。这一区分在理论上的重要性不亚于国内学者秦立志提出的"不确定性→威胁预期→安全困境"因果链(参见 [[秦立志 战略不确定性与安全困境 切卡|战略不确定性卡组]])：两者都揭示了一个使"信号-解读"模型失灵的机制性缺口。

值得注意的是，Lieberman 的"机动作为可信性解决机制"在依赖强度/表现性本体论的安全理论（如德勒兹式"事件"安全条件转换，参见 [[原始库提炼-事件·法律·安全条件转换]]）中获得了另一种诠释潜力：机动不是传递信号，而是**创造了新的条件场域**，在事件层面重新配置了安全关系的内在结构。
