# The power of the false (puissance du faux) is creatively falsifying: the falsifier in cinema (Welles, Robbe-Grillet) creates truth through falsification in a dialetheic structure of crystalline time

## 原文要点

> Deleuze on the falsifier: the falsifier is "the simulacrum, the sorcerer, the one who is full of metamorphoses and inventions, who says 'no' to the power of the false in order to give it a power which makes it into a creator." [...] The falsifier does not oppose truth but "brings forth the power of the false."

> Shores on the Strong Thesis: "Deleuze's godless or 'demonic' incompossibility — where multiple incompatible outcomes coexist without a divine arbiter — constitutes the strongest case for dialetheism in Deleuze's thinking."

> Crystalline time (C2): the time-image where "the real and the imaginary, the actual and the virtual, become indiscernible," creating a "crystal of time" — multiple temporally divergent series coexist simultaneously.

## 我的判断

Shores 在第七、八章处理德勒兹的"虚假的权力"（puissance du faux）这一重要但难解的概念，并以此作为 dialetheism 的最强论证。对德勒兹来说，虚假者（the falsifier）——如奥森·威尔斯的电影中的角色——不是说出错误的命题，而是通过创造性的歪曲（fabulation）来"创造真理"。这种歪曲不是与真理对立，而是通过歪曲产生了新的真理标准——这就是"虚假的权力"的含义。

Shores 的核心论证在时间-晶体（crystalline time）概念上达到顶点。德勒兹在《电影2》中提出，现代电影的时间-影像不再遵循 Chronos 的线性时间秩序，而是让真实与想象、现在与过去、实际与虚拟在同一个晶体结构中交替震荡，无法区分——这就是"时间脱节"（time out of joint）。Shores 认为这正是 dialetheic 结构：多重不兼容的时间系列共存于同一"晶体"中，既非综合（synthesis）也非选择（selection），而是真实的共存。这是我见过的时间-晶体与 dialetheic 逻辑之间最清晰的关联性论证。但 Shores 也诚实地说这一点仍然需要到他的第三卷才能充分展开（涉及语言哲学）。
