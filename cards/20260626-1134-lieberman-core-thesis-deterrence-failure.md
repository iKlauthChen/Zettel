# 累积威慑+拒止→消耗陷阱→威慑失败：Lieberman 核心论题

## 原文要点

> Israeli policymakers have relied on cumulative deterrence strategies to combat terrorism. However, Israel has consistently failed to deter Hamas' attacks, not only on October 7 but also in 2008-09, 2012, 2014, and 2021. A critical yet often overlooked observation is that cumulative deterrence strategies coupled with robust denial capabilities can lead to an attrition trap, which serves as a victory strategy for weaker actors, ultimately resulting in deterrence failure rather than success.
>
> ...
>
> This paper asserts that Hamas remained undeterred throughout the conflict because Israel viewed reliance on cumulative deterrence strategies and its robust denial capability, the Iron Dome, as less costly than engaging in a war of maneuver, which was essential to resolving Israel's credibility issue regarding its willingness to act.

> Furthermore, investment in denial mechanisms in isolation from other strategies leads not to deterrence success but to further failure... In the Hamas case, we find that the Iron Dome also led to deterrence failure because, paradoxically, its military success enabled Israel to tolerate the less costly rounds of warfare. Successful denial undermined the need to contemplate costlier methods to reestablish deterrence.

> The present argument, which suggests that 'deterrence is increasingly about practicing denial,' ought to be replaced by the argument that deterrence is about moving from cumulative deterrence, relying on tactical denial and punishment that targets the cost calculus of the challenger, to the use of force that targets the adversary's winning strategies.

## 我的判断

Lieberman 的核心论题对当前"拒止威慑"(deterrence by denial)的主流论述构成了有力的系统性反驳。边际主义者(Marginalists)认为拒止(denial)已取代惩罚成为反恐威慑的基石(Wilner 2021)，但 Lieberman 的纵向案例研究揭示了一个反直觉机制：**成功的拒止能力(铁穹)恰恰降低了国家升级为地面机动的政治意愿，从而剥夺了解决可信性问题的唯一手段**。我称此为"拒止-威慑悖论"(denial-deter paradox)。

这一论点与 [[原始库提炼-剩余作为跨域模型|剩余安全模型]] 的"意识形态质询的不完全性"有深层共振：铁穹作为技术性拒止手段，其"成功"掩盖了威慑关系的结构性缺陷，正如剩余安全框架中生产性安全体制的"成功"掩盖了剩余主体的不可化约性。两者的共同盲区是：**将战术层面的有效运作等同于战略层面的稳定结构**。

但 Lieberman 的工具箱本身有局限性：他始终在理性威慑框架内部运作(成本-收益计算、信号传递、可信性)，没有触及威慑关系自身的本体论前提——威慑者的主体位置如何在每一次"成功"中被重塑。这是该论证可被德勒兹/拉康式安全研究进一步深化的接口。
