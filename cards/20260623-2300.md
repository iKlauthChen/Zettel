# Žižek's core thesis in *Less Than Nothing*: materialist ontology must begin not from Being but from the "less than nothing" — reality is less than nothing, supplemented by fiction to conceal its emptiness

## 原文要点

> "Less Than Nothing endeavors to draw all the ontological consequences from this eppur si muove. Here is the formula at its most elementary: 'moving' is the striving to reach the void, namely, 'things move,' there is something instead of nothing, not because reality is in excess in comparison with mere nothing, but because reality is less than nothing. This is why reality has to be supplemented by fiction: to conceal its emptiness."

> Higgs field analogy: "What we get here is the physical version of how 'something appears out of nothing'... a something that contains less energy than nothing, a 'something' that is characterized by an overall negative energy."

> The drive: "This book tries to demonstrate that the Freudian drive cannot be reduced to what Buddhism denounces as desire or to what Heidegger denounces as the Will: even after we reach the end of this critical overcoming of desire-will-subjectivity, something continues to move. What survives death is the Holy Spirit sustained by an obscene 'partial object' that stands for the indestructible drive."

> Four contemporary ideological positions: (1) scientific naturalism, (2) discursive historicism, (3) New Age Western Buddhism, (4) transcendental finitude (Heidegger). "There is a dimension missed by all four, that of a pre-transcendental gap/rupture, the Freudian name for which is the drive."

## 我的判断

Žižek 的核心论题——"少于无"（less than nothing）——不仅是一个耸人听闻的标题，而是一个精确的本体论主张。传统的 ontology 问题是"为什么有某物而不是无"——回答通常是"因为有（某物），无是不可能的"。Žižek 翻转了这个逻辑：不是因为实在比无更多，而是因为它比无更少，所以才有运动（eppur si muove——"然而它动"）。希格斯场的物理类比起着关键作用：希格斯场出现在被抽空的容器中并进一步降低能量——它含有比"无"更少的能量。这对应于弗洛伊德的"驱力"（drive/Trieb）：驱力不是指向满足的冲动，而是指向"空"（void）自身的追求——它比"零"更少，是一个"负面能量"的持存。

这个论点的战略意义在于同时拒绝四个当代意识形态立场：（1）科学自然主义认为实在"就是那样"；（2）话语历史主义认为实在由话语建构；（3）佛教认为我们可以超越欲望到达空的平和；（4）海德格尔认为主体的意志是问题而"放下"（Gelassenheit）是出路。Žižek 说这四种都错过了"驱力"——那个在欲望/意志/主体消散后仍在"运动"的东西。这一点直接连通黑格尔的"否定之否定"：不是回到肯定，而是"无的否定"，是从无到无到无的运动。这与我对"剩余安全"的思考有直接共鸣：安全不是"避免失去"（减少损失→零），不是"恢复原状"（回到某物），而是那"少于无"的持续运动——一种无法被归零的剩余性持存。
