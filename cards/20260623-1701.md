# Deleuze's "paradox of pure becoming" is best understood through Priest's dialetheic account of motion: at the instant of change, a moving object both is and is not at a position

## 原文要点

> Deleuze writes of Alice: "Certainly, she is not bigger and smaller at the same time. But it is at the same time that she becomes both. She is bigger now; she was smaller before." [...] "It is at the same time, in the same stroke, that one becomes bigger than one was and one is made smaller than one becomes. This is the simultaneity of a becoming whose characteristic is to elude the present."

> Priest's dialetheic account: "Consider a body in motion. [...] To be in motion at an instant, then, according to this account, is to have an inconsistent state description at that instant. Objects in motion are at one place at one time, and another at another. But this is not sufficient. [...] To be in motion at a time, an object must both be and not be at a place at that time."

> "Dialetheism allows time to be both inconsistent and real."

## 我的判断

Shores 第一章的基本策略是通过对比 Bergson 的运动直觉（运动不可分割、无法被空间化）与 Russell 的"at-at"运动理论（运动即在不同时间点占据不同位置），引出 Priest 的 dialetheic 运动理论——在极小时间间隔内，运动物体既在某个位置又不在该位置（"spread hypothesis"）。然后，Shores 论证德勒兹在《意义的逻辑》（Logic of Sense）中通过 Alice 变大变小的悖论表达的"纯粹生成"（pure becoming），实际上具有相同的逻辑结构：在生成的瞬间，Alice 既比她过去的体型大又比她未来的体型小。但这里有一个关键差异，Shores 没有完全解决：Priest 的 dialetheic 模型依赖于极小区间的"扩散"（spread），而德勒兹的"纯粹生成"是"躲避现在"（eludes the present）的——它不占据任何确定的时间点。这意味着德勒兹的生成悖论可能比 Priest 的 dialetheism 更激进：它不是"既 P 又非 P 在同一个现在"，而是"生成永远不在现在中发生"。这个张力恰恰是德勒兹时间哲学的独特之处。
