# The central thesis: Deleuze's basic logical principles are best formulated as a dialetheic, many-valued system with truth-value "gluts"

## 原文要点

> The main purpose of this book is to formulate the basic principles of Gilles Deleuze's logic by means of the concepts and tools made available by contemporary logics. [...] The ultimate purpose of this book is to support the more controversial claim that Deleuze was a dialetheist whose logic is best formulated using a many-valued system with truth-value "gluts."

> David Lapoujade: "Deleuze is interested foremost in logic [...] Deleuze is above all a logician and all his books are 'Logics.' His first book on Hume could have been called 'Logic of Human Nature,' just as his book on Proust could have been called 'Logic of Signs.' [...] Likewise, the books on cinema could have been called 'Logic of Images,' just as there are a Logic of Sense and a Logic of Sensation."

> Shores' own contribution: "to explore and apply certain systems of non-classical logic to Deleuze's philosophy more directly, extensively, and comprehensively than has ever been done before."

## 我的判断

Shores 的核心论题极具争议性但也非常诱人：德勒兹不是反逻辑的，他反对的只是古典逻辑（同一律、不矛盾律、排中律），而他的哲学预设了一种非古典的、允许真值"过剩"（gluts，即命题既可真又可假）的多值逻辑——dialetheism。这一主张的激进之处在于，它不满足于说德勒兹的哲学"可以使用"非古典逻辑来描述，而是声称德勒兹"本人就是 dialetheist"。Shores 的论证策略是通过与 Graham Priest（dialetheism 的当代领军人物）的系统性对话来建立这一点——Priest 的运动理论、gluon 构成理论、非传递性同一性等，都被用来阐明德勒兹的纯粹生成悖论、异质性组合和概念一致性。但我注意到 Shores 诚实承认德勒兹本人从未正式阐述过这种逻辑：实际上德勒兹在多值逻辑方面"没有投入太多思考"。因此 Shores 的策略不是文本学意义上的还原，而是"如果德勒兹当时知道这种逻辑，他可能会使用它来阐明自己的思想"。这既是这本书的原创性所在，也是其脆弱性所在。
