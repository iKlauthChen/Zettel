# Lefebvre's spatial triad (perceived-conceived-lived) and the homogenization-differentiation dialectic of abstract space: the critical theory of urbanization

## 原文要点

> Lefebvre's spatial triad: (1) perceived space (espace perçu) of spatial practice — material infrastructures; (2) conceived space (espace conçu) of representations of space — scientific, technocratic knowledge; (3) lived space (espace vécu) of spaces of representation — art, play, everyday meaning.

> "Abstract space is not homogenous; it simply has homogeneity as its goal, its orientation, its 'lens'." The production of space proceeds through "a relational process of homogenisation-differentiation."

> Lefebvre: "capitalism has found itself able to attenuate (if not resolve) its internal contradictions for a century... by occupying space, by producing a space."

> Lefebvre on state planning violence: "the 'plan' does not remain innocently on paper. On the ground, the bulldozer realizes 'plans'."

## 我的判断

Charnock 对列斐伏尔空间三元组（感知空间-构思空间-生活空间）和抽象空间的说明简洁而准确。关键点是抽象空间（abstract space）的"同质化-差异化"辩证：抽象空间的目标是同质性（可交换、可量化），但它的实际运作制造差异（中心和边缘、投资热点和被抛弃区域）。这意味着空间的"抽象性"不是哲学抽象而是具体的社会过程——列斐伏尔称之为 mondialisation（世界化），即世界变得全球化和城市化。他还指出列斐伏尔的批判与法兰克福学派的关键区别：阿多诺和霍克海默的"完全被管理的社会"（totally administered society）和"理性之蚀"（eclipse of reason）虽然准确描述了趋势，但作为总体（totality）是一面性的。列斐伏尔坚持认为日常生活是"累积与非累积的交汇点"——革命的可能性存在于资本未能完全吸纳的剩余（residuum）。这对我的剩余安全概念的另一个方向（capital produces space but also produces unassimilable remainder）非常有启示：安全问题的"剩余"不仅是逻辑性的，也是在资本主义空间生产过程中被制造出来的。
