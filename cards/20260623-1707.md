# Philosophical thinking is transdisciplinary concept-creation whose consistency is Dupréelian (heterogeneous parts held together by tension) rather than logical (non-contradiction)

## 原文要点

> Deleuze's transdisciplinary philosophy: concepts from different disciplines (art, science, philosophy) encounter each other not through analogy but through a "rivalry of friends" — they "throw out" to each other "a ball that cannot be caught except by changing the rules of the game."

> Dupréel's concept of consistency: "two things that are united by their differences, not by their similarities." The hold between heterogeneous parts is a "tension" (tension) — the parts "repel each other and also attract each other, which is precisely the condition of their consistency."

> Deleuze's definition of concepts: they have "endoconsistency" (internal cohesion of components) and "exoconsistency" (relations with other concepts). A concept is never created alone but "always in conjunction with other concepts."

## 我的判断

第三章相对轻松，处理德勒兹的"思想作为跨学科概念创造"的理论。Shores 使用了一个令人难忘的图像：德勒兹展示当没有想法时，"头脑像弹球机一样工作"——没有预定路径，弹珠不断被撞击改变方向。这与杜普雷（Dupréel）的一致性理论高度契合：两个异质性部分不是通过相似性而是通过张力（tension）联结，它们既互斥又互吸——这恰好是弹球机中注意力（弹珠）在不同概念域之间弹跳的逻辑。Shores 认为，杜普雷一致性的"特殊逻辑"——异质性通过张力而非同一性结合——本质上是 dialetheic 的：概念的不同组成部分之间并不是逻辑一致的（在经典意义上），但它们以一种非排斥的、保持差异的方式共存。这为"思想"（thinking）作为创造性活动提供了一种逻辑模型：不是从给定前提出发进行演绎，而是在异质性要素之间的张力中创造新的连结。这个结论呼应了德勒兹的核心主张："哲学在于概念的创造"——而创造的前提不是逻辑同一性，而是杜普雷式的 tension 一致性。
