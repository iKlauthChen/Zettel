# The ontology of quantum physics: the two vacuums (classical void vs quantum vacuum) confirm the Hegelian-Lacanian logic of the "less than nothing" and the "barred Real"

## 原文要点

> Two vacuums: "The ontological problem of quantum physics is not that of how something emerges out of nothing, but of how nothing emerges out of something." The classical void is "empty space"; the quantum vacuum is a "seething tumult of virtual particles popping in and out of existence." The latter is "less than nothing" — energy fluctuations below zero point.

> Knowledge in the Real: Žižek argues, drawing on the "participatory" interpretation of quantum mechanics (Wheeler, von Neumann), that "knowledge is inscribed into the fabric of the Real itself" — the observer's intervention is not merely epistemological but ontologically constitutive. This parallels Hegel's "transposing an epistemological obstacle into the thing itself."

> "Y'a de l'Un" (Lacan): "There is something of the One" — but this One is not primordial unity; it emerges as the result of an operation, a "counting" that presupposes a minimal gap. In quantum terms, the One emerges from the quantum vacuum fluctuations.

> Agential realism (Barad) critique: Žižek argues that quantum physics does not support a neo-materialist "entanglement" ontology (Barad, Bennett) but rather the Hegelian-Lacanian logic of the barred Real — the Real is not an indivisible One of entanglement but a "cracked" non-All.

## 我的判断

全书最后一章处理量子物理的哲学含义，是 Žižek 最被低估但也是最勇敢的理论贡献之一。他提出的"两虚空"（two vacuums）论直接支撑了"少于无"的本体论：经典虚空是"空的空间"（某物被移除后留下的无），而量子真空是"沸腾的虚粒子海洋"（比零更少的能量波动）。后者对应拉康的"条纹化的实在界"（barred Real）——实在界不是空的而是一个充满最小差异（minimal difference）的场域。Žižek 对 Neo-materialist "纠缠"（entanglement）本体论的批判特别重要：巴鲁德（Barad）的"能动实在论"（agential realism）和本内特（Bennett）的"活力物质论"（vibrant matter）都基于对量子物理的一种特定解读——万物互联、没有割裂。Žižek 说这种解读错过了一个关键点：量子物理揭示的不是整体性，而是根本的不连续性——量子跃迁、观测行为的干预、波函数坍缩——这些都是切割而非连接。"y a de l'Un"（有某一）不是原始的统一，而是计数（counting）的效果，计数已经预设了一个最小缺口。这回到了全书的根本论点：存在的基础是"裂缝"（crack/antagonism），而不是"一"的和谐整体。恩格斯说"唯物主义"对 Žižek 来说是"一个正在运作的阴影"——物质不是一个满实的在场，而是一个永远不会完全显现自身的空框架。
