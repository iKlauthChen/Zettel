# Deleuze's ungrounding (effondement): thought starts not from ground but from groundlessness, and the movement of ungrounding itself becomes a new kind of ground

## 原文要点

> Deleuze's entire project can be seen like Heidegger's as one long answer to the post-Kantian question of how to ground thought. For both, thinking is groundless. [...] Building on Nietzsche they both call "earth" a realm where life under industrial modernity could be revalorized. But where Heidegger thinks under the sway of a pagan sort of poetics and emphasizes the possibilities of a post-religious sacred in the here and now, Deleuze is more consistently critical and atheist and stays much closer to Nietzsche's refusal of transcendence and identity.

> Deleuze's ungrounding is antifoundationalist but not nihilist. [...] Championing immanence means that thinking is immanent to its ground. Thinking is not directly determined by the ground, earth, or matter. The earth is present at the beginning as a problematic genetic field that thinking actively and messily deals with. [...] For Deleuze, thought surfs along spacetime as the realm of infinite plasticity.

## 我的判断

Saldanha 清晰地勾勒出德勒兹"奠基瓦解"（ungrounding/effondement）的理论谱系：从康德的有限性革命（主体使客体围绕自身转动）到海德格尔的无根性（Abgrund），再到德勒兹的对无根基性的积极肯定。关键在于德勒兹不是从"没有基础"滑向虚无主义，而是把"拒绝超越性、拒绝走出洞穴"这一运动本身当作新的基础——不是静态的根基，而是无限的内在性平面上的漂移。这个论证特别有地理学意义：当德勒兹说"哲学在拥有历史之前就有地理学"时，思想的方向和轴线（axes and orientations）先于其时间发展。这与海德格尔的根本分歧在于，海德格尔的无根性最终重新锚定在种族、土地和血液的"本真性"上（"错误的民族、土地和血液"——WP 109），而德勒兹则始终拒绝任何关于起源和家园的乡愁。对我而言，这意味着"剩余安全"如果要走德勒兹路线，就不能诉诸任何形式的"扎根"或"家园"修辞——安全不能被想象为回归某种失落的原初状态。
