# Intuitionism and negationless mathematics provide Deleuze with a logic of problems, undecidable propositions, and positive difference without negation

## 原文要点

> Brouwer's intuitionism: mathematical truth is mental construction from the "basic intuition" of time. The Principle of Excluded Middle is invalid because some propositions (e.g., "123456789 appears in π's decimal expansion") can be neither constructively proven nor disproven.

> Deleuze writes: "the third world of the excluded middle is as much the object of a 'problem' in the mathematical sense as it is the object of a 'problem' in the Marxian sense." Developing from Samir Amin, he links the "included third" to the relation between center and periphery in global capitalism.

> Griss's negationless mathematics constructs difference as "distance" (afstand) rather than as a logical negation of identity: "equality of numbers can be defined as 'having the same (or shared) differences,' and the inequality of numbers can be defined as 'having at least one difference.'"

## 我的判断

第六章处理直觉主义（intuitionism）——Brouwer 的数学构造主义。对德勒兹来说，直觉主义最重要的贡献是"排中律无效"：由于数学真理取决于我们的构造能力，某些命题（如 π 的十进制展开中是否存在某个序列）既不能被构造性证明也不能被反证，因此 A ∨ ¬A 不一定成立。这就是"非决定命题"（undecidable propositions）的概念。Shores 正确地指出了德勒兹对直觉主义的"包含第三项"（included third/included middle）命题的挪用——德勒兹把这一逻辑概念用于政治经济学（中心-边缘关系：第三世界既不在中心也不完全在边缘，而是"被包含的第三项"）。

但更有意思的部分是 Griss 的"无否定数学"（negationless mathematics）。Grise 试图构建一个完全不使用否定符号的数学系统，其中差异不是通过否定同一性（"A 不是 B"）来表述的，而是通过"距离"（distance）：两个数的差异被定义为它们共享的"不同之处"（differences）而非逻辑否定。Shores 认为这可能是与德勒兹的"差异的肯定性"最接近的逻辑系统——差异不是同一性的缺乏或否定，而是正面的距离本身。不过 Shores 也指出无否定数学严格来说不是一种逻辑而是一种数学方案，因此它不能完全替代 dialetheic 逻辑作为德勒兹逻辑的形式化工具。
