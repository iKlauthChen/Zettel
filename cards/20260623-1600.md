# Saldanha: Deleuze's spatiality operates across four registers — Earth, Flows, Places, Maps

## 原文要点

> Deleuze is a highly original thinker who cannot be easily classified in one philosophical tradition or another. [...] Running through it are four clusters of presuppositions. First, space might be the most important of all concepts. [...] Second, though there are many reasons internal to theoretical tradition for an explicit concept of space, it is more interesting to understand how and why theory is in fact forced to conceptualize space by its own situatedness. [...] Third, like most twentieth-century thinkers Deleuze does not have an extensive philosophy of space, despite having elaborated one of the most original modern philosophies of time. [...] Fourth, this book will affirm that space is real, not the mental category it is for Kant. [...] Space is difference, multiplicity, change, and movement, not some separate formal realm that would frame them.

> Space "after" Deleuze can mean three things. First, what space is according to Deleuze. Second, the Deleuze-event in theorizing forces us to rethink old categories with new philosophical tools. Third, theory evolves, and Deleuze cannot be our only or final inspiration.

## 我的判断

Saldanha 的四大进路——Earth/Flows/Places/Maps——构成了一种从宏观到微观、从物质到虚拟、从本体论到认识论的空间化递进。Earth 处理思想本身的扎根（grounding/ungrounding），是后康德内在性哲学的德勒兹版本；Flows 处理人口、资本与战争的运动学，对应德勒兹与加塔利的游牧学与政治经济学；Places 处理界域化（territorialization）的机制——条纹化、身体、城市与事件；Maps 则上升到虚拟/强度的本体论层面，把制图学重新定义为对差异本身的追踪。这种四分结构有意避开了传统地理学的区域/地方/景观分类，转而以德勒兹自身的概念簇为组织原则。Saldanha 明确指出德勒兹没有空间哲学但有时间哲学，因此这套框架不是"应用"德勒兹而是从德勒兹的隐伏维度中"提取"空间性。对我而言，最关键的是第四层：空间不是康德式的先天直观形式，而是差异、多重性、变化的物质性本身——这意味着空间性不能从社会过程中被"抽象"出来作为分析框架，它就是过程的物质形态。
