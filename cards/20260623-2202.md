# A geography of complex spaces: Bonta-Protevi translate Deleuzian spatial concepts (stratified/smooth, territorialized/deterritorialized) into the language of nested attractors, emergence, and self-organizing landscapes

## 原文要点

> Emergence defined: "an emergent structure is that which enables focused systematic behavior through constraining the action of component parts." This creates "reciprocal causality" — the mutual constitution of "local-to-global or 'upward' causality" and "global-to-local or 'downward' causality."

> Stratified space: "systems locked into deep basins of attraction; strong habitual patterns for matter." The organism is a stratum — "that which life sets against itself to limit itself" (ATP 503).

> Smooth space: the plane of consistency where "unformed matters" and "nonformal functions" interact in far-from-equilibrium conditions. Consistencies preserve the heterogeneity of components operating in a "sensitive condition."

> Olancho case study (Appendix): Honduras space where arborescent systems (state logging concessions, NGO governance) and rhizomatic systems (local cattle smuggling, informal economies) — multiple abstract machines coexist and interfere.

## 我的判断

Bonta-Protevi 的地理进路不同于 Saldanha 的城市/事件/全球化地理学。它更接近于"生态学"或"地景科学"——将人类社会空间理解为嵌套吸引子系统，层次不仅是社会分层的比喻，而是物质系统突现的功能性约束。该书的"复杂空间地理学"核心概念是：**突现结构通过约束组分的行动来实现聚焦的系统行为**——这是自上而下因果性（downward causation），与自下而上的产生性因果性（upward causation）形成互构。层次（strata）是锁定在深吸引子中的系统（强惯性的物质模式），而一致性（consistency）是远离平衡态、保持异质性组分的自组织。

奥兰乔（Olancho，洪都拉斯）的案例研究提供了一个具体的"地缘哲学分析"样本：该地区同时有国家层面的林业特许经营权和 NGO 治理（树状系统）与地方性的牛只走私和非正式经济（根茎系统）并行运作。这两种系统对应于不同的抽象机器——一个是编码/界域化/捕获的机器，一个是解码/解域化/连接的机器。它们在同一物质空间中交织，形成"纠缠的空间"（entangled spaces）。对我而言，奥兰乔案例展示了一种非常实用的德勒兹-加塔利"工具箱"应用：不是宏大理论叙事，而是通过分析多重抽象机器的共存和干扰来理解特定地缘政治空间——这种"多尺度"（nested attractors）分析方法对理解灰色地带和剩余安全的空间性非常有直接参考价值。
