# The critique of Lefebvre: Smith and Kerr argue that Lefebvre's treatment of nature as external substratum reproduces the dualism he claims to overcome

## 原文要点

> Smith's critique: Lefebvre "leaves nature largely unreconstructed" — "nature is reduced to a substratum." The production of space, for Smith, "is integral to the production of nature and fashioned within it. It is the corollary of the production of nature rather than the other way round."

> Kerr's critique: "Class struggle and history are reduced to abstract time and exist in the container of abstract space, while this space has contradictions of its own which can then externally 'envelop historical contradictions'."

> Kerr: "It is the capital relation that continually attempts to subordinate the whole (space) of society to the abstract logic of linear time, the ticking of the factory clock. This abstract time is not the concrete history of capitalism, but rather is the dominant and contradictory tendency through which that history expresses itself."

> Smith's political question: "where Lefebvre closes in on the political conclusions of his analysis, ... space seems to fall entirely out of the picture."

## 我的判断

Charnock 选编的 Smith 和 Kerr 对列斐伏尔的批判是本章最有理论张力的一部分。核心指控是：列斐伏尔声称要克服空间/自然、空间/社会的二元论，但实际上他把自然还原为"基质"（substratum）——被动的、无时间性的背景——这使得他无法理解"第二自然"（second nature）的完整含义。Smith 认为空间的生产不是对自然生产的替代，而是后者的衍生物（corollary）。Kerr 的批判更加根本：列斐伏尔把阶级压迫从时间转移到空间，再用空间的矛盾"包裹"历史矛盾，但真正驱动资本主义的不是空间的时间化或时间空间化，而是资本关系本身——它同时试图把空间和历史从属于抽象劳动时间的量化逻辑。这意味着列斐伏尔的空间转向可能确实过于激进，以至于丢失了马克思政治经济学的核心。Smith 的最终问题很尖锐：当你需要指出政治出路时，"空间从画面中完全消失了"。这似乎暴露了空间本体论的内在局限——如果一切都被生产为空间，那么在政治上具体要改变什么？这个问题对包括德勒兹空间哲学在内的所有"空间转向"理论都有警示意义。
