# Both Deleuze and Luhmann theorize sense (Sinn) as immanently creative: nonsense/noise is not the absence of sense but its constitutive outside and creative reservoir

## 原文要点

> Deleuze: "Nonsense does not have any particular sense, but is opposed to the absence of sense rather than to the sense that it produces in excess." Nonsense "enacts the donation of sense."

> Luhmann: "[A]ll meaningful operations always reproduce the presence of what has been excluded, for the world of sense is a complete world, which can exclude what it excludes only itself. Non-sense, too, can therefore be thought and communicated only in the medium of sense, only in the form of sense."

> Sense is the "fourth dimension of the proposition" (Deleuze) — beyond the idea expressed (subject), the material referent (object), and the logical relation between both. Sense emerges when these three dimensions interact; it is "never originary but always caused and derived."

> Luhmann's complexity (noise) is equivalent to Deleuze's nonsense: both are a chaotic multiplicity that drives sense-making from the inside, not an external ground.

## 我的判断

Richter 对德勒兹《意义的逻辑》和卢曼《社会系统》的意义理论的"折叠"是全书最精彩的理论建构。两侧共享的关键论断是：意义是彻底内在的创造，其驱动力不是外在于意义的来源（上帝、理性、物质、主体），而是意义表达本身内部产生的"外部"——对德勒兹是无意义（nonsense），对卢曼是复杂性/噪杂（complexity/noise）。Richter 将德勒兹的 Stoic 准因果（quasi-causes, incorporeals）理论——身体混合产生新的非物体，这个非物体才是真正的创造因——与卢曼的 Spencer Brown "形式律"（Laws of Form）和再入（re-entry）概念结合起来：每一次意义表达都画出一条区分线，把已被意义化的内部从尚未意义化的外部中区分出来，但这个外部是区分行为本身产生的，并非预先存在。Richter 精准地指出德勒兹将无意义定位于主体心理（phantasm, Lacanian psychoanalysis），而卢曼将其扩展到社会系统——法律、经济、政治同样通过意义/噪杂的悖论运作。这为政治分析打开了新的维度。
