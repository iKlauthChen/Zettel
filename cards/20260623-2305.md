# The political suspension of the ethical: Žižek's traversal of Lacan yields a politics that rejects both Badiou's "evental" break and Heidegger's "Gelassenheit" in favor of the drive's persistence

## 原文要点

> Lardreau's diagnosis: "Lacan unveiled the illusions on which capitalist reality as well as its false transgressions are based, but his final result is that we are condemned to domination — the Master is the constitutive ingredient of the very symbolic order, so the attempts to overcome domination only generate new figures of the Master."

> Žižek's response: "The great task of those who are ready to go through Lacan is thus to articulate the space for a revolt which will not be recaptured by one or another version of the discourse of the Master."

> Critique of Badiou: Badiou's "event" is too much of a "miracle" — it lacks the "obscene" underside of the drive. Badiou tries to overcome Lacan's "antiphilosophy" but in doing so falls back into a philosophy of the One (the event as the truth-procedure that subtracts itself from the situation). What Badiou misses is the "minimal difference" that persists even in the wake of the event.

> Critique of Heidegger: the "turn" (Kehre) from will to Gelassenheit (releasement) misses the drive — "what survives the overcoming of the will is not Gelassenheit but the drive." Heidegger's authentic Dasein is replaced by the "there is" (es gibt) that still remains too "poetic."

> The political suspension of the ethical (conclusion): a genuine political act does not follow ethical norms — it suspends them in the name of the Real. "A revolution is not a moral act." This does not mean it is immoral but that it operates in the dimension beyond Good and Evil where the drive's "less than nothing" is the ultimate stake.

## 我的判断

全书的政治结论集中在"政治的伦理悬置"（The Political Suspension of the Ethical）中——一个看似挑衅性的论断，但实际上是全书本体论论证的逻辑结论。如果实在的基础是"裂缝"和"少于无"，那么任何严肃的政治行为都不能遵循现存的伦理规范——因为这些规范本身就是维持对"裂缝"的遮蔽而存在的。这里的关键对话者是拉尔朵（Guy Lardreau）和让贝（Christian Jambet）——他们从拉康出发认识到"主人是符号秩序的构成性要素"（任何反叛都会产生新主人），因而从激进政治退回到"内修"（inner spiritual revolution）。Žižek 拒绝这一撤退："经过拉康之后"（going through Lacan）不是通向政治绝望而是通向更彻底的政治——不是试图逃避主人话语，而是面对主人话语的真实代价并坚持"驱力"的不可消除性。

Žižek 对巴迪乌的批判集中在"事件过于奇迹化"：巴迪乌的事件是"从无到某物"，它作为一个真理程序的开始完全打破了既定情境。但 Žižek 说这漏掉了"从无到无再到某物"的过程——事件之后那个"凹坑"（objet a）仍在运作，驱力不是被"忠诚"驯化而是持存为忠诚的不安分内核。对海德格尔的批判则更直接：转向（Kehre）从意志到放下（Gelassenheit）实际上是对驱力的逃避——海德格尔的真实性（Eigentlichkeit）最终回到了"诗意栖居"的宁静乡愁。Žižek 的"唯物主义"驱力是一种不宁静的持存——不是在放下中超越意志，而是认识到意志本身已经是被驱力驱动的表象。革命不是道德行为——不是因为革命是坏的，而是因为革命在善恶之外的层面运作，那正是驱力所在。
