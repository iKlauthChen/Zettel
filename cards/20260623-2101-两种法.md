# Two logics of law: Oedipal law (Name-of-the-Father, hierarchical) and post-Oedipal law (sinthome law, dispersed through desire and jouissance)

## 原文要点

> Zevnik distinguishes between two laws: (1) The Oedipal/paternal law — "the law of the father with an imaginary — mythical — and never attainable authority." This law structures the subject through prohibition and the Name-of-the-Father. (2) The post-Oedipal law — drawing on the later Lacan (sinthome) and Deleuze, "the post-Oedipal law is dispersed and 'ordered' as a multitude or contingency."

> The sinthome law: "By opening up the law to ideas of desire as such and jouissance I try to illustrate a type of law that can facilitate the radical contingency of the Real within the law itself. I argue for radically unstable structures of law and ethical principles that no longer correspond to or formulate categories resulting in particular political programmes."

> Oedipal law is linked to the Cartesian subject (stable, sovereign); post-Oedipal law is linked to the monist ontology of the not-all (open, multiple).

## 我的判断

第一章的"两种法"区分是全书的理论基础。Zevnik 利用拉康晚期的"圣状"（sinthome）概念——拉康在讨论乔伊斯时发展出的第四个环（将实在界/象征界/想象界联结起来的替代父亲之名的东西）——来论证一种"非俄狄浦斯的法"的可能性。Sinthome 法不同于以禁止为基础的父法：它不是通过"不允许"来定义主体，而是通过欲望和 jouissance 的肯定性流动来组建存在形式。Zevnik 将这一拉康式后俄狄浦斯法与德勒兹的"游牧"和"战争机器"概念折叠：后俄狄浦斯法不是从上方或外部施加的等级制规范，而是从内在的欲望流中涌现的网络状秩序。这一区分的政治意义在于：如果关塔那摩被拘留者的主体形式是按照俄狄浦斯法（主权-法律-主体）来定义的，那么被排斥在这个框架之外——他们没有被承认为完整的法律主体——但他们仍然可以通过一种非俄狄浦斯法的"欲望-身体"表达来宣称自身的存在。这就是为什么 Zevnik 认为关塔那摩被拘留者的抵抗实践不能被简化为"要求被承认为法律主体"——它显示了另一种存在形式和另一种法律逻辑的可能性。
