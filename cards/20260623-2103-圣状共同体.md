# The sinthome community: Zevnik's vision of a new ontology for world politics through the Borromean knot, the sinthome law, and a reimagined concept of rights

## 原文要点

> Zevnik proposes the Borromean knot (three rings of Real-Symbolic-Imaginary linked by the sinthome as the fourth) as the "scheme of a new ontology of the political subject." The sinthome — following Lacan's reading of Joyce — is the element that holds the three registers together "without any one of them being privileged over the others."

> "The question of community or coming-together": Zevnik argues that a post-Oedipal politics based on the sinthome would produce a community organized not around a shared identity (the One) or shared lack (the split subject) but around "a shared relationship to the real of one's desire."

> On rights: "A different conception of rights might bear the capacity to rethink the existing notions of human rights or the subject of law... Rights would no longer be attached to a fixed subject-position but would emerge from and respond to singular encounters with the real."

> The image of law that corresponds to the sinthome community is "a law that does not proscribe, prohibit or punish but follows the lines of desire and jouissance, a law that is radically open to the contingency of the real."

## 我的判断

第六章是全书的结论，也是最具争议性的部分——Zevnik 尝试勾勒一种与"非全一元论"相对应的政治共同体形态。她使用拉康晚期的博罗米结（Borromean knot）——想象界/象征界/实在界的三个环由"圣状"（sinthome）串联起来，没有哪个环节优先——来设想一种没有等级秩序的"联结"（coming-together）形式。圣状共同体的核心不是共同的认同（我们是谁）或共同的缺乏（我们缺少什么），而是对各自欲望的实在界的一种"共享关系"——这正是拉康"非全"的积极含义：每个人不是通过排除差异来联合，而是通过承认"每个人的实在界都不可被符号化全"来联合。Zevnik 对权利的重构也很有意思：权利不再是附着于固定主体位置（"人权主体"、"公民"）的法定资格，而是从与实在界的单一遭遇中产生的、不断变化的"表达"。这意味着权利不是已经给定的，而是通过每一次伦理遭遇被重新创造出来的。这给"剩余安全"提供了一个重要参照：安全权利不应当是预先确定的资格（"你有权安全"），而应当是从安全化实践与实在界的遭遇中涌现出来的临时性"出现"（appearance）。
