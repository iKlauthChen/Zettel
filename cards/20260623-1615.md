# Folding as the key topological figure: Deleuze's Leibnizian-Baroque ontology of space as origami, plenum, and the politics of cartography

## 原文要点

> "The division of the continuous must not be taken as of sand dividing into grains, but as that of a sheet of paper or of a tunic in folds, in such a way that an infinite number of folds can be produced, some smaller than others, but without the body ever dissolving into points or minima. [...] The unit of matter, the smallest element of the labyrinth, is the fold, not the point" (FLB 6).

> "The outside is not a fixed limit but a moving matter animated by peristaltic movements, folds and foldings that together make up an inside: they are not something other than the outside, but precisely the inside of the outside" (F 96-97).

> "A map has multiple entryways, as opposed to the tracing, which always comes back 'to the same'. The map has to do with performance, whereas the tracing always involves an alleged 'competence'" (ATP 12-13).

> Saldanha's concluding diagnosis: "Work is still to be done to think through how there must be transcendental syntheses of space for perception or cinema or thinking to happen at all."

## 我的判断

折叠（folding）是德勒兹最抽象但也最具空间性的概念。Saldanha 出色地展示了折叠如何在德勒兹的作品中运作于多个层面。在《表达主义与哲学》中，折叠是表达（expression）——宇宙在事物中折叠自身，事物展开（explicate）宇宙；在《福柯》中，折叠是主体化——外部通过折叠生成内部，自我不是内在性而是外部的内褶（"the inside of the outside"）；在《褶皱》（The Fold）中，折叠是莱布尼茨的满实宇宙——物质不自下而上地被组合，而是从曲线和倾侧（inflection）中涌现，折叠是物质的基本单位（"unit of matter"），比点更原始。这对空间理论决定性的意义在于：空间不是空的容器（牛顿），不是先天的直观形式（康德），甚至不是事物之间的关系——空间是事物的自我折叠和展开。

Saldanha 以一场关于制图学（cartography）的讨论结束全书。德勒兹-加塔利的"制图学"不是表征（tracing）而是表演（map——"multiple entryways"）——不是再现已存在的东西，而是参与到虚拟的差异场域中，不断修改自身。这种制图学直接对抗资本的表征主义（GPS，谷歌地图），但也提出一个问题：如果制图学永远是开放的和可修改的，它如何产生政治效力？

Saldanha 最后承认一个未完成的理论任务——"必须思考空间为了知觉、电影或思考而发生所需的先验综合"。这是一个诚实而开放的结束。
