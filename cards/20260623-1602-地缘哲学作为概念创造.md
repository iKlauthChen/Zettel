# Geophilosophy: philosophy as the creation of concepts from the earth's material unpredictability, not from transcendent categories

## 原文要点

> If philosophy is defined as the creation of concepts, geophilosophy is the name Deleuze gives at the end of his life to the creation of concepts as more-than-human and emergent from "the earth."

> As a dynamic system the planet continuously, and often ominously, ungrounds itself. It is this vortex of matter and energy that thinking after Deleuze tries to map. If concepts do not fall from above one of the responsibilities of the philosopher is to examine how concepts sprout within specific spatiotemporal conditions.

> "Geography wrests history from the cult of necessity in order to stress the irreducibility of contingency. It wrests it from the cult of origins in order to affirm the power of a 'milieu'" (WP 96).

> "Philosophy takes the relative deterritorialization of capital to the absolute; it makes it pass over the plane of immanence as movement to the infinite and suppresses it as internal limit, turns it back against itself so as to summon forth a new earth, a new people" (WP 99).

## 我的判断

Saldanha 正确强调了地缘哲学（geophilosophy）的双重含义：其一，作为后康德内在性哲学的别名——思想不从超越性标准出发，而从物质性的内在平面出发；其二，作为对概念本身的地理历史分析——概念总是从特定的时空条件中萌发（如希腊哲学从爱琴海盆地涌现）。但最关键的是第三点：地球本身就是解域化（deterritorialization）。在《反俄狄浦斯》（AO 140-141）中，地球不仅是"原始的生产要素"，还是"无器官身体"——所有生产之前已经存在的虚拟领域。这意味着地球不是思想的被动背景，而是思想的积极触发条件：它迫使思想去应对其物质性的不可预测性。但 Saldanha 也坦诚地指出德勒兹和加塔利没有摆脱欧洲中心主义："只有西方扩展和传播其内在性中心"（WP 97）——他们仍然认为亚洲只有"形象"（myth, mandala）而非真正的概念。这是一个值得批判却往往被德勒兹信徒忽略的局限。
