# Three kinds of lines (rigid, supple, flight) and four dangers organize all social assemblages and their virtual cartography

## 原文要点

> "In a book, as in all things, there are lines of articulation or segmentarity, strata and territories: but also lines of flight, movements of deterritorialization and destratification. Comparative rates of flow on these lines produce phenomena of relative slowness and viscosity, or, on the contrary, of acceleration and rupture" (ATP 3-4).

> "We are therefore made of three lines, but each kind of line has its dangers. Not only the segmented lines that cleave us, and impose upon us the striations of a homogeneous space, but also the molecular lines, already ferrying their micro-black holes, and finally the lines of flight themselves, which always risk abandoning their creative potentialities and turning into a line of death" (ATP 506).

> "All the dangers of the other lines pale by comparison" to the line of flight turning into a line of destruction: "Like Kleist's line of flight, and the strange war he wages; like suicide, double suicide, a way out that turns the line of flight into a line of death" (ATP 229).

## 我的判断

德勒兹的"线"理论是他最接近系统化社会空间图绘的方法。Saldanha 概括了三条线——刚性分段线（rigid segmentarity：国家、阶层、性别的二元分割）、柔性分子线（supple molecular：部落的流动分段、游牧的灵活组织）、逃逸线（lines of flight：解域化的创造性冲动）——以及与之对应的四种危险：刚性线的封闭（法西斯的全序化）、柔性线的微黑洞（微型法西斯的自我强化）、两种线合流的权力（totalitarianism——刚性官僚+柔性欲望的共谋），以及最具毁灭性的第四种危险：逃逸线向毁灭线（line of destruction/death）的转化。这最后一种危险对应于弗洛伊德的死亡驱力，但德勒兹与弗洛伊德不同——死亡线不是回归无机物的惯性，而是欲望本身的模态：克莱斯特的"生命计划"中包含了自杀作为逃逸线的极端形式。Saldanha 准确地指出德勒兹不是在浪漫化疯狂或自杀，而是在展示"即使是逃逸，也需要极端的谨慎"（"minimum of strata"——ATP 270）。这种谨慎本身就是分子政治的核心伦理。
