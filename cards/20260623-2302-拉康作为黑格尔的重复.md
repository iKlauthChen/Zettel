# Lacan as the repetition of Hegel: suture, objet a, and the logic of "not-all" (non-All/pas-tout) constitute Lacan's contribution to a materialist ontology of the drive

## 原文要点

> Suture: "the elementary mechanism of the signifying process, the mechanism which enables us to understand Lacan's definition of the signifier as 'that which represents the subject for another signifier.'" The subject emerges as the "lack" in the signifying chain — it is "represented" only through the gap between signifiers.

> Objet a: "the object generated by the signifying process" — not a positive object but the "void" around which desire circulates. It is the "partial object" that embodies the drive's "less than nothing." The voice and the gaze are paradigmatic: they are not objects in the world but the "object" that falls out of the field of the visible/audible.

> Formulae of sexuation: "The All with an exception" (masculine) — all subjects are castrated except one (the primal father who enjoys without limit). "The non-All" (feminine) — there is no exception, but not every subject is submitted to the phallic function; "woman is not-all" means she is not fully covered by the symbolic order. This is not a biological claim but an ontological one: there are two modes of relating to the lack.

> "There is a non-relationship" — sexual difference is not a binary complementarity; it is the real of an impossible relation. Lacan's "there is no sexual relationship" means there is no direct inscription of sex in the symbolic — the gap is irreducible.

## 我的判断

Žižek 的"拉康作为黑格尔的重复"（repetition in Kierkegaard's sense）是全书最有理论张力也最有阐释力的部分。他不是说拉康"应用了"黑格尔或"受黑格尔影响"，而是拉康在精神分析的语境中重新做了黑格尔在德国观念论中所做的事情：把"缺失"（lack）从认识论障碍转化为事物本身的构成性裂缝。缝合（suture）是关键机制：能指链的运作产生了一个"空"（主体），而这个空又通过被缝合回能指链来掩盖其生产性——这就是"能指是代表主体为另一个能指的东西"的逻辑含义。小对形（objet a）则是在这个过程中被"排除"的"剩余"——它既不是主体也不是符号秩序的一部分，而是那个使两者之间的非关系（non-relationship）得以持存的"无"。嗓音和凝视作为 paradigmatic 的 objet a 意义在于：它们不能在世界中被定位为对象——"看"的行为有一个"从它自己眼中掉出来的斑点"，"听"的行为有一个无法被符号化的声核。

Žižek 强调的性化公式（formulae of sexuation）不是性别身份的断言，而是两种不同"缺位"的本体论模式：男性的"全除某外"（有一个父亲-享乐的例外）和女性的"非全"（没有例外但并非所有人都隶属于阳具功能）。女性的"非全"直接连通拉康晚期的"非整个"（pas-tout）本体论：实在界不是全的，总有缺口，总有东西没有被符号化捕获。这与黑格尔的"统一体中的裂缝"（裂缝在事物本身中）完全一致。但 Žižek 也指出拉康的局限：他的晚期工作最终陷入困境——圣状、纽结等尝试都是面对这一僵局的失败补救。
