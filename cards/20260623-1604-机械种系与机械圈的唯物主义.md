# Machinic phylum and mechanosphere: Deleuze-Guattari's anti-vitalist, anti-holistic materialism for the Anthropocene

## 原文要点

> The "machinic phylum" is what constructs a state: "the phylum simultaneously has two different modes of liaison: it is always connected to nomad space, whereas it conjugates with sedentary space" (ATP 409). [...] "the machinic phylum is materiality, natural or artificial, and both simultaneously; it is matter in movement, in flux, in variation, matter as a conveyor of singularities and traits of expression. This has obvious consequences: namely, this matter-flow can only be followed" (ATP 409).

> "There is no biosphere or noosphere, but everywhere the same Mechanosphere" (ATP 69). [...] Deleuze and Guattari want to emphasize the fragmented, frictional, automatic, or even mechanical nature of the biosphere and globalization. Like lava, like cog wheels, strata always already interlock, but they do not form unified spheres with their own impetus towards some divine apex of complexity.

## 我的判断

"机械种系"（machinic phylum）和"机械圈"（mechanosphere）是德勒兹-加塔利思想中最具当代意义的两个但也是最被忽视的概念。机械种系指穿越所有三个大层（物理化学层-有机层-人类层）的创造性物质流，以冶金为代表——金属矿脉的分布、游牧工匠的迁徙、帝国的扩张，这三者不是类比关系而是同一物质流的不同表达。机械圈则是对生机论（biosphere, noosphere）的唯物主义替代：德勒兹和加塔利明确拒绝任何形式的整体性——无论是以 Gaia 理论为代表的生态整体论，还是以 Teilhard de Chardin 的"智力圈"（noosphere）为代表的进化目的论。Saldanha 正确地指出，替代"生机"（bio-）为"机械"（mechano-）的目的是抽掉任何神秘主义地毯：生命不是统一的力量，而是被其底层所穿越、被熵和死亡所渗透。这对 Anthropocene 政治的意义在于：德勒兹提供了一种既承认行星尺度的互联性、又拒绝任何和谐终局论的唯物主义框架。人类层不是进化高峰，而是可能正在制造自身灭绝的一个在统计上微不足道的扰动。
