# Scale is real but derived: Deleuze-Guattari's molar/molecular distinction avoids flat ontology's leveling of difference

## 原文要点

> The plane of consistency knows nothing of differences in level, orders of magnitude, or distances. It knows nothing of the difference between the artificial and the natural. [...] "the strata are spin-offs, thickenings on a plane of consistency that is everywhere, always primary and always immanent" (ATP 69-70).

> At the molecular viewpoint, all bodies have idiosyncrasies that are suppressed to obtain molar categories. The same system simultaneously exhibits molar tendencies towards order and identity and molecular tendencies towards differentiation and recomposition. [...] The molar is constructed from the molecular, literally enveloping it, and cannot exist without it. The molecular is autonomous and constitutes itself even when it is in response to and in order to escape the molar.

> Class consciousness does not suffice for revolution. "At the 'microscopic' level, what must happen, first of all, is a kind of direct changeover to communism, the abolition of bourgeois power in the sense that that power is embodied in the bureaucrat, the leader or the militant dedicated revolutionary" (MR 63).

## 我的判断

Saldanha 就尺度（scale）问题与德勒兹地理学中的"平面本体论"（flat ontology）进行了一场极为重要的辩论。DeLanda 的平面本体论认为一切物理过程都在同一层次上运行，这导致某些后德勒兹地理学家（Marston, Jones, Woodward 2005）宣称尺度概念已过时——因为嵌套层级（Russian-doll hierarchy）本质上是超越性的。Saldanha 的回应尖锐而正确：平面本体论混淆了虚拟与实际。在一致性平面上确实没有尺度或等级——但在实实际层面，组合体（assemblages）必然是层叠化的、有尺度的。由此引出摩尔/分子（molar/molecular）这一关键概念对：这不是尺度的二元对立（不是"大 vs 小"），而是两种不同的空间构成模式。摩尔是可识别的宏观聚合——阶级、民族、结构；分子是灵活的、游牧的、稍纵即逝的欲望形成。二者的关系是非对称的：分子先于摩尔，摩尔从分子中结晶出来，但分子不断从摩尔中泄漏。这意味着对德勒兹而言，革命不是打碎国家机器（摩尔对抗），而是分子层面的欲望重新布线——这解释了为什么德勒兹-加塔利在"分子革命是自发的"与"需要最低限度的中央集权"之间摇摆。
